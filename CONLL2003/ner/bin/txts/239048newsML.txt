CRICKET-PAKISTAN WIN TOSS, CHOOSE TO BAT VS NZ.

SIALKOT, Pakistan 1996-12-06

Pakistan won the toss and elected to bat in the second one-day international against New Zealand in Sialkot on Friday.

Pakistan leads the three-match series 1-0 after winning the first game at Gujranwala by eleven runs on Wednesday.

Teams:

Pakistan - Saeed Anwar, Zahoor Elahi, Ijaz Ahmad, Inzamamul Haq, Salim Malik, Moin Khan, Wasim Akram (captain), Shahid Afridi, Saqlain Mushtaq, Mushtaq Ahmad, Waqar Younis.

New Zealand - Bryan Young, Craig Spearman, Adam Parore, Stephen Fleming, Chris Cairns, Nathan Astle, Justin Vaughan, Simon Doull, Chris Harris, Lee Germon (captain), Robert Kennedy.
