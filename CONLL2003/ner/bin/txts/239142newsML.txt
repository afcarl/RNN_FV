FIVE DIE AS CROP PLANE COLLIDES WITH PICKUP.

JOHANNESBURG 1996-12-06

Five people were killed and one slightly injured when a crop-spraying airplane crashed into a light delivery vehicle while preparing for takeoff in South Africa's North West region, state radio reported on Friday.

The freak accident occurred in Mafikeng on Thursday. The pilot survived the crash, but the driver and passengers of the bakkie were killed.
