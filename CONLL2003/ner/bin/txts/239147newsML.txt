HUNGARY RADIO NOON HEADLINES - DEC 6.

BUDAPEST 1996-12-06

These are the main headlines on Hungary's Kossuth Radio midday news. Reuters cannot vouch for their accuracy:

- Budapest's electricity distributor Elmu posted one billion forint loss, due to the delay in energy price increases.

- A 310-strong Hungarian contingent will travel to Bosnia on December 18.

- The police will start a series of action to catch criminals robbing passengers on international express trains.

- RMDSZ, the party of ethnic Hungarians in Romania hesitates to enter the ruling coalition.

- Electric energy industry trade unions plan protests in Brussels and Essen because they said Germany's RWE, an investor in the Hungarian energy sector, had not fulfilled its wage increase promise.

-- Budapest newsroom +36 1 327-4040
