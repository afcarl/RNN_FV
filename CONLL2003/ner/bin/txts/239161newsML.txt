NATIONAL BANK OF HUNGARY STATEMENT.

BUDAPEST 1996-12-06

The National Bank of Hungary (NBH) said in a statement on Friday that the bank will work on Saturday December 21 but will be closed on Tuesday December 24.

On Friday December 20 NBH employees will work to their Tuesday schedule and on Saturday December 21 they will follow the Friday schedule, the statement said.
