Finsbury International Hedge sets premium.

LONDON 1996-12-06

Shares in Guernsey-based investment fund Finsbury International Hedge Investment Company set a modest premium on their first day of trading on London Stock Exchange on Friday.

The shares, placed at US$10 by Rea Brothers and SBC Warburg, were quoted at US$10.15 by 1600 GMT, although no trade was registered in the stock.

The placing valued the fund, managed by International Asset Management, at US$100.0 million.
