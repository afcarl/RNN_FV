EBRD backs Warsaw agricultural market with loan.

LONDON 1996-12-06

The European Bank for Reconstruction and Development said on Friday it would lend $15 million to the Warsaw Agricultural Wholesale Market Co.

The 12.5 year, sovereign-guaranteed, loan will goes towards improving trading and storage facilities for agricultural suppliers and producers in the Warsaw region, which accounts for over 30 percent of the country's fruit production and 10 percent of its vegetable output.
