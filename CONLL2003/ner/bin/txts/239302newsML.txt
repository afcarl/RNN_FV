Versatech acquires Tarxien Corp.

TORONTO 1996-12-06

Versatech Industries Inc said on Friday they had acquired 98.8 per cent of the issued and outstanding shares of the Tarxien Corporation for US$35.5 million.

The acquisition was completed by a tender offer through Gecamex Technologies Inc.  Gecamex is a 75 per cent owned subsidiary of Versatech.

Versatech engineers, designs and manufactures products for the automotive industry.
