RESEARCH ALERT - Circuit City estimates cut.

-- EVEREN Securities Inc said Friday it lowered its third quarter earnings estimate for Circuit City Stores Inc to $0.20 from $0.25.

-- EVEREN said Circuit City recently reported November same store sales fell by eight percent, less than the 10 percent decline it had projected. However, these better than expected sales levels were achieved at the expense of margins, it added.

-- It also cut the earnings estimates for fiscal 1997 to $1.35 from $1.50 and the estimate for fiscal 1998 to $1.60 from $1.75.

-- Circuit City shares fell two Friday at 30.
