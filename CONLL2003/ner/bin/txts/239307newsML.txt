RESEARCH ALERT - Best Buy estimate lowered.

-- EVEREN Securities Inc Friday lowered its estimate for Best Buy Inc's third quarter earnings to $0.03 a share from $0.15.

-- It also reduced estimates for Best Buy's fiscal 1997 earnings to $0.45 from $0.70 and fiscal 1998 earnings to $0.70 from $0.83.

-- Best Buy shares lost 3/8 Friday to close at 12-7/8.
