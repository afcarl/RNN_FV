Voice it Worldwide hires altbrandt as CEO.

FORT COLLINS, CO. 1996-12-06

Voice It Worldwide Inc said it hired Dennis Altbrandt as chief executive to replace Michelle Morgan, who leaves the position to assume additional sales and marketing responsibilities.

The move is effective immediately, the company said.

Morgan will remain president of the company. She assumes some of the the responsibilities of Patricia Westbrook, a vice president of sales and marketing who resigned.

Altbrandt has worked with the company as a consultant since February in finance, strategy and general management.
