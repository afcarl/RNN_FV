Poore Bros shrs up on first trading day.

GOODYEAR, Ariz. 1996-12-06

Poore Brothers Inc's shares closed on Friday at 4-1/2, up one from their IPO price of $3.50.

In a statement, the company said its initial public offering of 2.25 million shares was priced at $3.50 each.

Paradise Valley Securities Inc underwrote the offering, the company said. The company produces, markets and distributes salty snack foods that are sold primarily in the southern and western United States.
