AutoZone promotes Adams to chief executive.

MEMPHIS, Tenn. 1996-12-06

AutoZone Inc said on Friday that Johnston Adams Jr. was assuming the title of chief executive officer from J.R. Hyde III.

Hyde will continue to serve as chairman.

Adams, previously chief operating officer, will be elected president and chief executive officer at the company's board meeting on December 12, 1996, AutoZone said.

Vice Chairman Timothy Vargo will be named to the additional post of chief operating officer, the auto parts retailer said.
