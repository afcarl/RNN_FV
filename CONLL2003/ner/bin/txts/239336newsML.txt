Gordon Getty group cuts Noble Drilling stake.

WASHINGTON 1996-12-06

A group led by San Francisco investor Gordon Getty said Friday it reduced its stake in Noble Drilling Corp to 4.6 percent, or 5,576,690 common shares.

In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, the group said that P.A.J.W. Corp, in which Getty is sole shareholder, sold 3.0 million common shares of Noble between November 21 and 26.

The group, which includes the Ann and Gordon Getty Foundation, did not disclose how much it received for the shares.

Noble Drilling, based in Houston, is a major drilling contractor, with offshore and land operations in the United States, Canada, Mexico, Britain, Africa, the Middle East, South America and India.
