Spacetec names Neil Rossen CFO.

LOWELL, Mass. 1996-12-06

Spacetec IMC Corp said on Friday that it had named Neil Rossen chief financial officer and senior vice president.

Rossen replaces Linda Linsalata, who resigned to pursue other opportunities, the company said.

Prior to joining Spacetec, Rossen was with a private telecommunications firm, a spokesman for the company said.
