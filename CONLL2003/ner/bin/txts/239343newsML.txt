Group holds 7.1 pct class A Weitzer stake.

WASHINGTON 1996-12-06

A group led by Morris Propp, a New York investor, said Friday it holds 168,000 class A common shares of Weitzer Homebuilders Inc, representing 7.1 percent of the class A outstanding.

In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, the group said the stake includes 88,100 shares it bought between November 6 and Decmeber 2 at prices ranging from $1.5625 to $1.8125 a share.

The group said it holds the stake in the Miami Lakes, Fla.,-based company for investment purposes.
