AFTER THE BELL - After-hours activity stays in line.

NEW YORK 1996-12-06

Traders said after-hours activity on Friday was moderate but no stocks traded significantly off their closing prices.

Wall Street was busy during market hours as stocks largely dropped in response to comments made by Federal Reserve chairman Alan Greenspan.

The New York Stock Exchange said its session one volume was 10,500 shares compared to 39,800 shares on Thursday. Session two volume was 3,998,200 shares compared to 574,752 shares on Thursday.
