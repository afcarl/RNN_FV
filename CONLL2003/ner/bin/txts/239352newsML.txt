Berkshire raised stake in McDonald's.

CHICAGO 1996-12-06

Berkshire Hathaway Inc raised its stake in McDonald's Corp to 9.34 million shares as of September 30, 1995, it disclosed in a recently Securities and Exchange Commission filing.

In March, it was disclosed that Berkshire, headed by Omaha, Neb., billionaire Warren Buffett, bought 4.96 million shares of McDonald's sometime during the last quarter of 1994.

According to the SEC filing, the 9.34 million shares were valued at about $357.3 million.

McDonald's declined to comment on Berkshire's stake.

In the third quarter of 1996, McDonald's listed its average shares outstanding at 697.8 million.
