Amerindo group cuts PSINet stake to 11 pct.

WASHINGTON 1996-12-06

A group including Amerindo Investment Advisors Inc said Friday it reduced its stake in PSINet to 11.00 percent, or 4,408,930 common shares.

In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, the group said it sold 598,500 shares of PSINet between October 17 and November 27 at prices ranging from $9.00 to $14.0750 a share.

Amerindo is a San Francisco-based investment advisory firm.

Based in Herndon, Va., PSINet provides turn-key corporate Internet and intranet access.
