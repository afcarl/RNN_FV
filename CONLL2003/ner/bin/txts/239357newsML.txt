CME says its GLOBEX volume Thursday set record.

CHICAGO 1996-12-06

The Chicago Mercantile Exchange said Friday its volume on the overnight GLOBEX session Thursday reached a record 37,452.

Total GLOBEX volume for the session was 96,052, with volume on the Paris-based Marche a Terme International de France (MATIF) accounting for 58,600 contracts.

The CME's previous overnight GLOBEX record was 29,509.

The CME said Eurodollar futures led the overnight session, with 24,899 contracts traded. The CME, Reuters Holdings Plc and MATIF are partners in GLOBEX, an after-hours electronic trading system.
