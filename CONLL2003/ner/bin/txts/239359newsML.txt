Liz Claiborne terminates acquisition.

NEW YORK 1996-12-06

Liz Claiborne Inc said the previously announced agreement in principle covering its acquisition of the Dooney & Bourke Cos had terminated due to the failure of the parties to agree upon definitive terms.

Liz Claiborne said it will continue to evaluate and pursue acquisition opportunities which meet its strategic objectives.

Dooney & Bourke manufactures and markets handbags and leather goods.
