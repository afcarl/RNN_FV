Platinum, Innovative in marketing deal.

OAKBROOK TERRACE, Ill. 1996-12-06

Platinum Technology Inc and Innovative DP Designs Inc have signed a worldwide marketing agreement, effective immediately, the two companies said Friday.

The deal allows Platinum to co-market Innovative Designs' IMS Toolkit database utilities, and Innovative Designs to co-market Platinum's DB2 database utilities.

Terms of the agreement were not disclosed.

The database utilities are designed for database administrators and computer programmers.

Platinum provides software, and Innovative Designs provides database tools.
