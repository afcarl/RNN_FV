Forrester Research completes IPO.

CAMBRIDGE, Mass. 1996-12-06

Forrester Research Inc said on Friday that it completed an initial public offering of 2.3 million shares of its common stock at $16 a share.

All of the shares were offered by the company and included an option exercised by the underwriters to purchase an additional 300,000 shares to cover over-allotments.

The issue was managed by Goldman, Sachs & Co and Robertson, Stephens & Co.

Forrester, an independent research firm said the net proceeds from the offering would be used for working capital and general corporate purposes.
