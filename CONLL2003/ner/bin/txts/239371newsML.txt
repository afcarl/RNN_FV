NCH says to pay special div of $1.00/shr.

IRVING, Texas 1996-12-06

NCH Corp on Friday said it will pay a special dividend of $1 per share on December 16 to shareholders of record on December 2, 1996.

NCH will also pay its regular dividend of $0.30 cent a share on the same date.
