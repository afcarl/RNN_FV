Computer Integration CEO Farrell resigns.

BOCA RATON, Fla. 1996-12-06

Computer Integration Corp said on Friday that Ronald Farrell has resigned as chief executive officer so he could devote more time to his financial consulting firm.

Samuel McElhaney was elected to succeed Farrell as chief executive officer of the company, Computer Integration said.

Farrell, who was not re-elected as chairman, entered into a consulting agreement with the company, Computer Integration said. Farrell will remain on the board of directors, the company said.  

A chairman will be named at the next board meeting in January, Computer Integration said.

McElhaney was previously executive vice president of the company's CIC Systems unit and was on the company's board of directors, Computer Integration said.
