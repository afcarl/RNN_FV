Dollar General names Krysiak president.

NASHVILLE, Tenn. 1996-12-06

Dollar General Corp said on Friday it has named Bruce Krysiak president, succeeding Cal Turner Jr.

Turner will remain chairman and chief executive officer, Dollar General said.

Krysiak will assume his responsibilities as president in January 1997, the company said.

Prior to joining Dollar General, a discount merchandise retailer, Krysiak served as chief operating officer of Circle K Corp.
