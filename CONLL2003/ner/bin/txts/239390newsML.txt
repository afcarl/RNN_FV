NUKO stake cut by Dawson-Samberg group.

WASHINGTON 1996-12-06

A group led by Dawson-Samberg Capital Management Inc said Friday it cut its stake in NUKO Information Systems Inc to 637,500 common shares or 6.1 percent from 8.7 percent held previously.

In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, the group said its latest transactions include a net 268,100 common shares sold between Oct 3 and Dec 5 at prices ranging from $14 to $17.0417 per share.

The group holds the stake for investment purposes.
