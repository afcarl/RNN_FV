ACT Networks launches new FX-1000 product.

CAMARILLO, Calif. 1996-12-06

ACT Networks Inc on Friday unveiled its FX-1000 product, the first of its new FrameXchange product line that boosts the ability of telecommunications carriers to handle voice traffic.

"The FX-1000 is the first product specifically designed for central-office-based applications," Martin Shum, chief executive officer, said in a statement.

The FX-1000 is available for evaluation by carriers.

ACT shares closed up 2-1/2 at 29.
