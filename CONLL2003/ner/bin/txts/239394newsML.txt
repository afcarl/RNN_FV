Noodle Kidoodle names CFO.

FARMINGDALE, N.Y. 1996-12-06

Noodle Kidooodle Inc said on Friday that it has appointed Kenneth Betuker vice president and chief financial officer, effective December 9, 1996.

Betuker replaces William Johnson, who is leaving the company to pursue other interests, the company said.

Betuker was previously executive vice president, chief financial and administrative officer and treasuer at First National Supermarkets Inc, the company said.
