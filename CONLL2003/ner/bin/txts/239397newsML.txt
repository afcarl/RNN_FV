Caribbean Cigar signs exclusive agreement.

NEW YORK 1996-12-06

Caribbean Cigar Co said that it signed an exclusive distribution agreement with Southern Wine & Spirits of Florida which is expected to generate annual sales of $5 million.

Under the terms of the deal, Southern Wine & Spirits, a Miami-based division of Southern Wine & Spirits of America Inc, would exclusively distribute six Caribbean Cigar brands.
