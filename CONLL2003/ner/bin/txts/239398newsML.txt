Multimedia Games files for offering.

TULSA, Okla. 1996-12-06

Multimedia Games Inc said Friday that it has filed a registration statement with the Securities and Exchange Commission for the sale of about 2.3 million shares of common stock by various stockholders.  

The gaming manufactureer said it will not receive any of the procceds from the sale.

Multimedia shares rose 3/8 to 5-3/4 Friday.
