U.S. sells 1,220 tonnes surplus lead.

WASHINGTON 1996-12-06

The Defense National Stockpile Center said Friday it sold a total of 1,220 tonnes of surplus lead at an average price of $0.3197 per pound.

The lead was being sold to five firms.

Prices for the lead ranged from a low of $0.3161 per pound for two lots of 20 tonnes sold to Ney Smelting & Refining of Brooklyn, NY, and Parks Pioneer Metals Co., of Milwaukee, Wisc, to a high price of $0.3285 per pound for a total of 100 tonnes sold to Amerway, Inc. of Altoona, Pa.  

The next bid opening for lead is Thursday, Jan 2, 1997, the agency said.
