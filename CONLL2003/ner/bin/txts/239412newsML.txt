Individual Investor to go to Nasdaq Nat'l.

NEW YORK 1996-12-06

Individual Investor Group Inc, a financial information services company, said Friday its common stock, which has traded on Nasdaq SmallCap Market since 1991, will begin trading on Nasdaq National Market Dec 9.

Individual Investor said it was accepted for listing after meeting the more stringent quantitative and non-quantitative requirements of the Nasdaq National Market.

The company's ticker symbol will remain the same.
