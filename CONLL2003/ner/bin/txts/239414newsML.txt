Phillips unit plans Mexican joint venture.

BARTLESVILLE, Okla. 1996-12-06

Phillips Petroleum Co said Friday its Phillips Driscopipe division intends to enter into a joint venture with a Mexican firm to make high-density polyethylene pipe in Mexico.

Terms were not disclosed.

Phillips and Promotora Mexicana de Industrias SA de CV, a unit of Grupo Miguel, plan to make more than 20 million pounds of pipe a year for Mexican markets from March 1997.

The new firm will be named Driscopipe Mexicana SRL. Promotora Mexicana will hold a 51 percent interest, while Phillips Driscopipe will hold the remaining 49 percent.
