Home Financial sets first cash payout.

SPENCER, Ind. 1996-12-06

Home Financial Bancorp said Friday it declared its first cash dividend of $0.05 a share.

The dividend will be payable January 2, 1997, to holders of record on December 19.

Home Financial is the holding company for Owen Community Bank.
