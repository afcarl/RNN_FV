U.S. reserve assets fell in November.

WASHINGTON 1996-12-06

U.S. Treasury Department said Friday that U.S. reserve assets fell $113 million in November to $75.44 billion from $75.56 billion in October.

U.S. holdings of foreign currencies fell to $38.49 billion in March from $38.77 billion in October.

Gold stocks totaled $11.05 billion in both months.

Special Drawing Right with International Monetary Fund rose to $10.39 billion in November from $10.23 billion in October.

The value of the U.S. reserve position with IMF was virtually unchanged in November at $15.52 billion.
