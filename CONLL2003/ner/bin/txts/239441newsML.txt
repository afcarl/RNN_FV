Mavesa begins exchange offer for ADS.

NEW YORK 1996-12-06

Venezuela's Mavesa S.A., which makes, markets and distributes consumer goods, said Friday it had begun an exchange offer for its outstanding restricted Rule 144-A American Depositary Shares.

Institutions holding these shares will be able to exchange them for securities which trade freely, the company said. The offer will expire January 6, it said.

Each ADS represents 20 shares of Mavesa common stock.
