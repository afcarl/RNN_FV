MVSI sees Q1 EPS 'at least double' yr ago.

VIENNA, Va 1996-12-06

MVSI Inc said Friday that it expects earnings in the fiscal first quarter, which ends December 31, to "at least double" to $0.03 per share from the year-ago quarter on revenue about $9 million, compared with $3 million a year earlier.

The company did not say what factors contributed to the improved numbers for the quarter. Officials from the company were not immediately available to comment.

The company added that it expects to be profitable for the year ended September 30, with revenue of about $16 million compared with $2 million a year earlier. It also said it expects revenue of about $50 million in fiscal 1997.
