Taiwan Equity sees '96 loss, to skip payout.

JERSEY CITY, N.J. 1996-12-06

Taiwan Equity Fund Inc said Friday it expects net investment and net capital losses for fiscal 1996 and will not declare an annual shareholder distribution.  

The fund said its board had determined there is insufficient income to declare a 1996 distribution.

The fund said it has net assets of about $54.4 million and has a net asset value of $12.08 per share, based on 4,507,318 shares outstanding.

The fund was off 1/4 at 10 Friday afternoon.
