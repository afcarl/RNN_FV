First Virtual plans IPO of 3 mln shares at $11-$13.

NEW YORK 1996-12-06

First Virtual Holdings Inc FVHI.O has provided details of its planned initial public offering in a regulatory filing, registering for an offering of three million shares at an estimated price of between $11 and $13 each.

Bear Stearns & Co Inc is the lead underwriter of the offering, which the brokerage said is slated for sometime next week. Cowen & Co, Lehman Brothers and Unterberg Harris are also underwriting the offering.  

The San Diego company has developed software called VirtualPIN, which allows users to make secure financial transactions over the Internet by means of a personal identification number, without exposing their credit card numbers.

First Virtual currently has about 2,650 merchants and 180,000 consumers registered.
