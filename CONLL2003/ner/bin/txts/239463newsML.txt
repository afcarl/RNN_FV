Winland Electronics wins $5.5-mln contract.

MANKATO, Minn. 1996-12-06

Winland Electronics Inc announced Friday it received a contract to supply mobile communications equipment to PeopleNet Communications Corp.

The multi-year agreement is worth $5.5 million in the first year, Winland said.

Winland will make base units that link long-haul trucks to trucking company fleet managers using satellites, cellular and paging systems. The first shipments are expected to be delivered in early 1997, the company said.

Winland Electronics designs and markets electronic and electro-mechanical products and assemblies.
