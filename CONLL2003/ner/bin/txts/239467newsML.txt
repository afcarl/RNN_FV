Complete Management completes public offer.

NEW YORK 1996-12-05

Complete Mangement Inc said on Friday a public offering of two million common shares at $13.75 per share and $25 million of eight percent subordinated debentures convertible into common stock had become effective.

The debentures were convertible into common stock at $16 per share, the company said.

Interest would be payable on the debentures May 15 and December 15, starting on May 15, the company said.

A non-management shareholder was also selling 500,000 shares of common stock as part of the offering, Complete Management said.

The deal was co-managed by National Securities Corp and Commonwealth Associates, the company said.
