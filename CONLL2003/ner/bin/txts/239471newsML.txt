Concord Energy names Deral Knight chairman.

JOURDANTON, Texas 1996-12-06

Concord Energy Inc said Friday that Deral Knight had succeeded Jerry Swon as chairman.

Swon announced his resignation as chairman at a November 21 board meeting, Knight said.

Knight has been chief executive at Concord since June 30. He is the founder of Knight Equipment and Manufacturing Corp, which was bought by Concord in May 1995.
