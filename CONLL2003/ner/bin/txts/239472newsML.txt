Alumax unit ups aluminum extrusion prices.

NORCROSS, Ga. 1996-12-06

Alumax subsidiary Alumax Extrusions Inc said on Friday it increased prices $0.04 per pound on all aluminum extrusion orders entered after December 9, 1996, and all shipments on or after December 23.

The price increase did not apply to seamless tube and pipe and Acc-U-Plate product prices, the company said.

Alumax Extrusion makes soft-alloy aluminum extrusions.
