Environmental Power sets special dividend.

PORTSMOUTH, N.H. 1996-12-06

Environmental Power Corp said on Friday that it has declared a $0.05 a share dividend, including a special dividend of $0.02 a share, payable December 19, 1996 to shareholders of record on December 13, 1996.

The company said the special dividend was declared, resulting from its favorable results so far in 1996.

The company's regularly quarterly dividend is $0.03 a share, it said.
