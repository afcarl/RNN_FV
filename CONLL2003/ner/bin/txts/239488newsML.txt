Marriott and Madison suspend management plan.

NEW YORK 1996-12-06

Marriott International Inc said it and Madison Hotel owner Marshall Coyne agreed to suspend their previously announced plan for Marriott to manage the Madison, which is in Washington.

Both parties had announced the plan in October, which included a slated $10 million renovation of the 353-room hotel.

Marriott Chairman J.W. Marriott said in a statement that the company and Coyne could not agree on final terms.

Marriott officials were not immediately available to comment.
