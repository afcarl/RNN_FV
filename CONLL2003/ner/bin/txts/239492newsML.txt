White House says Nov jobs data show solid growth.

WASHINGTON 1996-12-06

White House economic adviser Joseph Stiglitz said Friday jobs data for November showed the U.S. economy is moving ahead at "solid, sustainable levels."

"Today's report confirms that the economy is moving forward at solid, sustainable levels with low unemployment, moderate inflation, solid economic growth and all Americans increasingly sharing in the benefits of that growth," said Stiglitz, chairman of President Clinton's Council of Economic Advisers.
