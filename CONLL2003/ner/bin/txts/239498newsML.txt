Duff reaffirms ServiceMaster single-A-minus rating.

CHICAGO 1996-12-06

Duff & Phelps Credit Rating Co said it has reaffirmed its single-A-minus rating on senior notes of ServiceMaster LP after ServiceMaster's announcement on Thursday of a definitive agreement to buy Barefoot Inc.

Duff, in a statement dated December 5, said the action covers about $501 million of ServiceMaster's debt.

In the acquisition, ServiceMaster will offer Barefoot stockholders $16 per share either in cash or an equivalent amount of ServiceMaster shares. The transaction's aggregate value is about $230 million.
