Nigerian 28-day T-bill sold at average 11.6981 pct.

LAGOS 1996-12-06

Nigeria's Central Bank said on Friday it sold 4,645 million naira worth of 28-day treasury bills carrying a weighted average rate of 11.6981 percent.

Bids totalled 4,645 million and the true yield will be 11.8063 percent.

Last week's sales were 2,676 million naira worth of 28-day treasury bills at a weighted average rate of 11.6999 percent.

($1=80 naira)
