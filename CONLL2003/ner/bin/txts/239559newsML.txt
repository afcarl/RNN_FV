S.African average T-Bill rate 15.93 pct.

PRETORIA 1996-12-06

The average discount rate for South African three-month Treasury Bills rose to 15.93 percent at the latest weekly tender after rising to 15.71 percent last week, the Reserve Bank said.

Applications for the 700 million rand of bills on offer totalled 2.554 billion rand. The average price was 96.030 rand percent.

The average rate for six-month bills was 15.31 percent from 15.18 percent. Applications for the 200 million rand of bills on offer totalled 540 million rand. The average price was 92.365 rand percent.
