SAfrica money mkt shortage eases to 9.269 bln rand.

JOHANNESBURG 1996-12-06

The Reserve Bank said the South African money market shortage fell to 9.269 billion rand on Thursday from 9.739 billion rand on Wednesday.

Three-month NCDs were quoted at between 17.15 and 17.20 percent offered from between 17.10 percent and 17.20 percent on Thursday, while the 90-day BA rate was quoted in a 16.50 to 16.75 percent range from 16.45 to 16.75 percent previously.

-- Johannesburg newsroom +27 11 482 1003
