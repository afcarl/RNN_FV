SA Druggist unit buys S.Africa's NMA.

JOHANNESBURG 1996-12-06

South African Druggists Ltd said on Friday its unit Healthcare Management Holdings (Pty) Ltd had acquired NMA Administrators (Pty) Ltd with effect form October 1.

It did not mention the value of the transaction. SA Druggists said had the acquisition been effective at its last financial year ended August 31 its tangible net asset value per share would have decreased by 8.5 percent to 986 cents from 1,077 cents.

SA Druggists said the acquisition was of strategic importance as it linked the extensive provider network established by HMH with the medical aid patient base of NMA.

It said further information on the acquisition would be set out in its financial statements for the year ended August 31, 1997.
