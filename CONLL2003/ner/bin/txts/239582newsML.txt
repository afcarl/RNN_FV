Elandsrand says talks still in progress.

JOHANNESBURG 1996-12-06

Elandsrand Gold Mining Company Ltd said on Friday that, further to the cautionary announced on Nov 15, talks it had entered into were still in progress and, if successfully concluded, may effect its share price.

It urged shareholders to exercise caution in their share dealings pending a further announcement.
