Deelkraal cautions talks still in progress.

JOHANNESBURG 1996-12-06

Deelkraal Gold Mining Company Ltd said on Friday that, further to the cautionary announced on Nov 15, talks it had entered into were still in progress and, if successfully concluded, may effect its share price.

Deelkraal urged shareholders to exercise caution when dealing in its shares pending a further announcement.
