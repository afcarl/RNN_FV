PLI shareholders reject new issue plan.

WARSAW 1996-12-06

An extraordinary shareholders meeting of Polish wholesale and retail trader Polish Life Improvement SA (PLI) rejected management's proposal for new share issue, PLI said in a statement on Friday.

PLI's management earlier said it wanted to issue between one million and 3.5 million shares to be offered to selected institutional long-term investors.

PLI, which is listed on the parallel market of the Warsaw bourse, currently has 7,051,213 shares outstanding.
