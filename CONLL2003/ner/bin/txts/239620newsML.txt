Latvian Nov inflation 0.8 pct, yr/yr 14.4 pct.

RIGA 1996-12-06

Latvian inflation was at 0.8 percent on a year-on-year basis in November, giving a year-on-year rise in prices of 14.4 percent, the statistics committee said on Friday.
