Romanian leu 3,618 to dollar for Dec 10 - BNR.

BUCHAREST 1996-12-06

Romania's National Bank (BNR) announced the leu's reference rate at 3,618 to the dollar for December 10.

This reference rate was set on Friday as a weighted average based on daily reports of foreign exchange operators. It is a guidance rate used for statistical accounting purposes.

The leu's reference rate set on Thursday for Dec 9 is 3,615 to the dollar. It was 3,617 on Friday. -- Bucharest Newsroom 40-1 3120264
