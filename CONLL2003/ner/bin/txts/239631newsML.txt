Czech CNB-120 index rises 0.1 points to 745.3 pts.

PRAGUE 1996-12-06

The CNB-120 index, a broad daily measure of Czech equities, rose 0.1 points on Friday to 745.3 points, the Czech National Bank (CNB) said.

Six of the ten sectoral indices rose, with the investment fund index rising the most, up 49.1 points to 1,165.1.

-- Prague Newsroom, 42-2-2423-0003
