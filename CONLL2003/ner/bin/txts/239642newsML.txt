C.Europe's CESI drops to 1,391.02 points.

BUDAPEST 1996-12-06

The CESI Central European Share Index dropped on Friday to close at 1,391.02 points, down 8.18 points or 0.58 percent, the Budapest Stock Exchange said.

The index, which has been published since February 1, reflects trends of 73 shares on the Budapest, Prague, Warsaw, Bratislava and Ljubljana bourses.
