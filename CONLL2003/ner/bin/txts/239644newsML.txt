Russian Pallada GKO fund assets 373.7 mln rbls.

MOSCOW 1996-12-06

Pallada Government Securities Unit Investment Fund, which started operating on November 12, said on Friday its net assets totalled 373,705,330 roubles.

A unit in the fund costs 10,000 roubles, a fund spokesman said.
