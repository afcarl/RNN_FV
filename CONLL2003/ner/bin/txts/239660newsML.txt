Czech BICS100 bond index stagnates at 101.888 pts.

PRAGUE 1996-12-06

The BICS 100 index measuring the Czech Republic's most liquid bonds remained unchanged in Friday's trading at 101.888 points, bank Ceska Sporitelna said.

It said the duration of the index portfolio shortened by 0.003 points to 2.480 years.

The BICSG 100 index, which Ceska Sporitelna calculates using only government bonds, stagnated at 98.443 points, the bank said, and the duration of the index portfolio shortened by 0.002 points to 2.353 years.

It said the yield index YBICS 1000 fell 0.037 points to 1,102.731.

-- Prague Newsroom, 42-2-2423-0003
