Croatian stocks down, Kras chocolate sole gainer.

ZAGREB 1996-12-06

Croatian stocks closed lower on Friday on small volume, with chocolate-maker Kras resisting the downward trend.

* Of eight active issues, seven were down and one up.

* Volume almost halved at 2,393 shares, turnover also lower at 1.4 million kuna.

* The only gainer Kras rose 20 kuna to close at 430, selling 724 shares.

* Top decliner hotelier Riviera Porec lost 44 kuna to 106, with 255 shares changing hands.

* Most active was Zagrebacka Banka with 815 shares traded. It fell 10 kuna to 990. -- Zagreb Newsroom, 385-1-4557075
