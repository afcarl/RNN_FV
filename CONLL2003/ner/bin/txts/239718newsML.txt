Slovak c.bank crown/dollar fix at 31.434 (31.643).

BRATISLAVA 1996-12-06

The National Bank of Slovakia (NBS) fixed the crown at 31.434 to the dollar after Thursday's fixing at 31.643 and at 20.413 to the mark from 20.283.

The crown fixing is announced daily at about 1140 CET (1040 GMT) and is within plus/minus 5.0 percent of a dollar/mark basket midpoint calculated at 11:30:00 CET.

The above rates imply a deviation from the midpoint of -0.84 percent after a deviation of -0.72 percent on Thursday.

-- Bratislava Newsroom, 42-7-210-3687
