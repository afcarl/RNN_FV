Hungary 1-mo BUBOR rate 22.70 pct, 3-mo 22.31 pct.

BUDAPEST 1996-12-06

Hungary's Budapest Interbank Offered Rate (BUBOR) remained steady for both one- and three-month maturities, the National Bank of Hungary (NBH) said on Thursday.

The one-month rate was fixed at 22.70 percent, while the three-month rate at 22.31 percent, the NBH said.
