Romania average one-week rate falls to 51 pct.

BUCHAREST 1996-12-06

Romania's central bank said on Friday the average accepted rate for its one-week refinancing funds slipped to 51 percent from 52.64 percent last week.

The central bank's offer of 1.01 trillion lei, down from 1.25 trillion the previous week, was undersubscribed with three banks bidding a total 1.0 trillion lei at 51 percent.

The central bank minimum permitted rate was 51 percent, down from 52 percent last week.

-- Luli Popescu, Bucharest Newsroom 40-1 3120264
