Lukoil says 1996 dividend to be no lower than 1995.

MOSCOW 1996-12-06

AO NK LUKoil, Russia's biggest oil company, said in a statement on Friday that dividends on 1996 earnings for ordinary shares, adjusted for inflation, would be no lower than those paid for 1995.

It gave no further details.

LUKoil paid 200 roubles per ordinary share and 500 roubles per preferred share for 1995.

($1 = 5,521 roubles).

At 0900 GMT LUKoil ordinaries were $10.50, down from $10.60 at Thursday's close.
