Three Russian soldiers killed in blast accident.

MOSCOW 1996-12-06

Three Russian servicemen were killed and 12 wounded in an explosion on a training ground of the Volga military district on Friday, a Defence Ministry spokesman said.

The spokesman did not give any other details but Interfax news agency said the explosion happened in the dark when a military unit was heading for field exercises.

"Preliminary reports say that one of the soldiers accidentally stepped on a grenade," Interfax quoted a military source as saying.
