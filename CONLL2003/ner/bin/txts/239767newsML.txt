Venezuela CVG gold production rose 34 pct in 1996.

CARACAS 1996-12-06

Gold production at Venezuelan state-owned industrial holding Corporacion Venezolana de Guayana's (CVG) subsidiary Minerven rose 34 percent in 1996 to reach 3,486 kilogrammes, CVG figures obtained by Reuters showed.

Minerva's gold sales will top 13.98 billion bolivars ($30 million) by year end versus 7.5 billion bolivars at the end of 1995, the figures showed. Sales figures were in nominal bolivars.

-- Omar Lugo, Caracas newsroom, 582 505 2600
