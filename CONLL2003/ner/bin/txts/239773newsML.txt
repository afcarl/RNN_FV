Venezuela M-0 rises 2.84 percent in week to Nov 22.

CARACAS 1996-12-06

Venezuela's monetary base rose 2.84 percent, or 69 billion bolivars, to 1.311 trillion bolivars in the week to Nov 22, the central bank said.

At the beginning of 1996, base money stood at 873 billion bolivars.

-- Caracas newsroom, 582 505 2600
