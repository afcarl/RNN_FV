Venezuelan M-2 falls 0.93 pct in week to Nov 22.

CARACAS 1996-12-06

Venezuelan money supply, as measured by the monetary aggregate M-2, rose 0.93 percent or 43 billion bolivars to 4.727 trillion bolivars in the week to Nov 22, the central bank said.

At the beginning of 1996, M-2 was 3.530 trillion bolivars.

-- Caracas newsroom, 582 505 2600
