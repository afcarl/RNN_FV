Argentine Central Bank places $153 mln in repos.

BUENOS AIRES 1996-12-06

Argentina's Central Bank said Friday it placed $153 million in overnight repurchase agreements at nine percent against government bonds.

In the interbank market, overnight call money fell to 6.40 percent from 6.48 percent Thursday.

The U.S. dollar fell to 0.99950 pesos from 0.99984 pesos at the close Thursday.
