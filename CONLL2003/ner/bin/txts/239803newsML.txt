Banco de Mexico assigns secondary credits.

MEXICO CITY 1996-12-06

Banco de Mexico injected 3.385 billion pesos on Friday by assigning two credit auctions in the secondary market.

The credits were assigned as follows:

    CREDIT AUCTIONS   TERM    MAXIMUM  MINIMUM  AVERAGE

    2.385  bln pesos  5 days  30.82    30.50    30.69

    1.000  bln pesos  3 days  31.00    31.00    31.00

NOTE: Maximum, minium and average are percentages
