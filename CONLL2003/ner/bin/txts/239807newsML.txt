Paraguayan Jan-Oct imports via ports down 27 pct.

ASUNCION 1996-12-06

Paraguayan river ports handled 188,754 tonnes of imported goods from January to October, 27 percent less than the same period last year, the National Shipping and Ports Authority said Friday.

Import volumes have fallen due to the country's economic slowdown, the Authority said.
