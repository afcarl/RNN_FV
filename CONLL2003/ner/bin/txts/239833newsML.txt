Brazil's Unibanco names new board chief.

SAO PAULO 1996-12-06

Brazil's third largest private bank Unibanco has named Pedro Moreira Salles president of the bank's Board of Directors, a bank spokeswoman said.

Moreira Salles, one of the sons of banker Walter Moreira Salles, will replace Roberto Konder Bornhausen, who has reached the bank's statutory age limit of 63 years to serve on the board. He will take up his position in January.

Bornhausen will take over as the president of Unibanco Holding's Board of Directors.

Moreira Salles family is Unibanco's majority shareholder.
