Brazil net dollar inflows at $55.9 mln Dec 5.

SAO PAULO 1996-12-06

Preliminary figures for Brazil's commercial and financial foreign exchange contracts showed a $55.9 million surplus on December 5 in the commercial forex market, according to Central Bank data supplied to banks.

A banker with access to the figures said the central bank issued the following breakdown.

DOLLAR FLOWS ON DECEMBER 5

Exports: $176.1 million

Imports: $242.6 million

Financial buy: $280.3 million

Financial sell: $157.9 million

Surplus: $55.9 million

DECEMBER DOLLAR FLOWS UP TO DATE

Exports: $781.4 million

Imports: $726.1 million

Financial buy: $898 million

Financial sell: $553.1 million

Net surplus: $400.2 million

Note: the figures were rounded off.

-- Alexandre Caverni, Sao Paulo newsroom, 55-11-2485418
