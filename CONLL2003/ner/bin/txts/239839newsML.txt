Zedillo proposes new central bank deputy governor.

MEXICO CITY 1996-12-05

Mexican President Ernesto Zedillo on Thursday proposed Deputy Finance Minister Jose Sidaoui as the new deputy governor of the central bank, to replace Ariel Buira whose term is coming to an end.

In a statement, Zedillo's office said the nomination had to be approved by the Senate. Buira's three-year term ends on December 31 this year.
