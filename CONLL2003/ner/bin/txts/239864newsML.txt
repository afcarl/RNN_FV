China's first non-state bank opens second branch.

BEIJING 1996-12-05

China's first non-state bank, the China Minsheng Banking Corporation opened its second branch on Friday and said it plans to open two more this month, the Xinhua news agency said.

Minsheng Bank opened its second branch in Beijing, two months after setting up its first branch in the Chinese capital, it said.

It plans to open a branch in the southern city of Guangzhou on December 18 and one in Shanghai on December 28, it quoted bank officials as saying.

The bank, founded last January, is a public-holding bank institution with a registered capital of 1.38 billion yuan and is 85 percent owned by 2,000 private shareholders.
