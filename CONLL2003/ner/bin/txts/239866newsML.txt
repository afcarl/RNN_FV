S.Korea's Kumho China tyre plant opens.

BEIJING 1996-12-06

A $120 million tyre joint venture between South Korea's Kumho Co Ltd and China has started production, the official Xinhua news agency said on Friday.

The first phase of the Nanjing-Kumho Tyre Corp Ltd in Nanjing in the eastern province of Jiangsu would produce three million tyres a year, Xinhua said.

"The company will expand its manufacturing scale to meet the needs of China's automotive industry," the agency quoted a company director as saying.

The plant was China's largest tyre factory, Xinhua said but gave no further details.
