Digital opens ASEAN marketing centre.

SINGAPORE 1996-12-06

Digital Equipment Corp said on Friday it had opened a centre for inquiries and direct marketing for ASEAN customers.

The Association of South East Asian Nations (ASEAN) groups Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand, Philippines, Indonesia, Brunei and Vietnam.

The centre, DIGITAL ASEAN Link (DiAL), offers customers and partners in the region access to information on the company, it said in a statement.

DiAL is in Digital's ASEAN office in Singapore. It handles inquiries in various ASEAN languages.
