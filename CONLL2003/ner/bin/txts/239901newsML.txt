Solartech flotation seen 128X subscribed - sources.

HONG KONG 1996-12-06

The initial public offering of shares of cable maker and exporter Solartech International Holdings Ltd, which closed on Thursday, is expected to be about 128X subscribed, market sources said on Friday.

Dealing in the company's shares is expected to begin on December 12.

Solartech issues 125 million shares at HK$1.20 each to raise capital to fund expansion of its production in China.

The company produces cables and wires for electrical appliances and telecommunications products.
