HK's Karrie IPO seen about 60X subscribed - sources.

HONG KONG 1996-12-06

The initial public offering of shares of personal computer casings maker Karrie International Holdings Ltd is expected to be about 60 times subscribed, market sources said on Friday.

An official statement on the subscription results will be released on December 9 and dealing in the company shares is expected to begin on December 16.

Karrie offered 90 million new shares at HK$1.15 each to raise fund repaying short-term bank loans and leasing loans, to buy machinery and equipment, and to buy surface mount technology machines for the manufacture of printed circuit board assemblies.
