LG Merchant Bank closes US$50 mln FRCD.

HONG KONG 1996-12-06

LG Merchant Banking Corp's US$50 million floating rate certificate of deposit issue has been completed on a club deal basis, bankers said.

They said the facility has a final maturity of five years with a put and a call option in year three.

The issue is priced at par to float at 55 basis points over the three-month London Interbank Offered Rate.

Management fees are 39 basis points for participants.

Particpating banks include KDB UK, Bayeriche Landesbank Asia, ING Baring Securities Hong Kong, Korea First Bank, Sumitomo Finance Asia, Shinhan Bank and Baden-Wurttembergische Bank AG.
