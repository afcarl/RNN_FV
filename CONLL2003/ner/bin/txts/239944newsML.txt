Qatar air spaces closes for five hours on Saturday.

DOHA 1996-12-06

Qatari air space will close for five six hours on Saturday and Monday during the arrival and departure of Gulf Arab leaders who gather in Doha for their annual summit, officials said.

The leaders of the six-nation Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC) alliance are due to start arriving in Qatar on Saturday and officials said the airport would close for commercial airlines from 0800-1300 (0500-1000 GMT).

The December 7-9 summit ends on Monday when the airport would close from 1300-1800 (1000-1500 GMT). REUTER AF (CONTACTABLE ON 974-404444 ROOM 611)
