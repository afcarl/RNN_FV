Iran polymer output to reach one mln tonnes by 199.

DUBAI 1996-12-06

Iran's polymer production is expected to reach one million tonnes a year by 1999 from 500,000 tonnes in 1995, the official Iranian New Agency (IRNA) said on Friday.

It quoted Industry Minister Mohammad Reza Nematzadeh as saying Iran's annual domestic polymer needs stood at 700,000 tonnes.

He gave no further details.
