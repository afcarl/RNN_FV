Istanbul gold volume falls amid price volatility.

ISTANBUL 1996-12-06

Volume on the Istanbul gold exchange shrank on Friday as traders took to the sidelines because of volatility in international gold markets, dealers said.

Total turnover of 205 kilos was in lira terms, with 195 kilos traded in the afternoon.

Volume was 360 kilos on Thursday.

Gold closed at 1,236,000 lira per gram, up from Thursday's 1,231,800 lira, reflecting the trend in Europe.
