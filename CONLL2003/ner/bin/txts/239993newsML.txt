RESEARCH ALERT - Kmart bonds buy reiterated.

-- Gruntal & Co said Friday it reiterated its buy recommendation on Kmart Corp's bonds after the company on Thursday reported a 4.1 percent rise in consolidated same store sales in November.

-- "Kmart is now performing at a level more representative of where the market and its major competitors are performing," Gruntal said in a report.

-- "We are reiterating our buy recommendation on most of Kmart Corp bonds because of attractive yields, the majority of which are near 10 percent," Gruntal said.
