Texas/w Okla fed cattle market slow at $67 - USDA.

AMARILLO 1996-12-06

Trade was slow in the Panhandle area early Friday, USDA said. Slaughter steers and heifers were $1.00 per cwt lower in a limited test.

Feedlots reporting light inquiry from buyers. Sales reported on 3,900 head slaughter steers and 100 heifers; following weekly movement of 71,200 head.

Note - all cattle prices based on net weights FOB the feedlot after a 4 percent shrink.

Slaughter Steers -  Select and Choice 2-3 1150-1200 lbs 67.00.

Slaughter Heifers -  Pen Select and Choice 2-3 1050 lbs 67.00. Confirmed - 4,000
