Indonesia Telkom at 35-1/8 in New York.

NEW YORK 1996-12-06

PT Telekomunikasi Indonesia (Telkom) closed at 35-1/8 in New York on Friday. It recorded the day's low of 35 and the day's high of 35-3/8.
