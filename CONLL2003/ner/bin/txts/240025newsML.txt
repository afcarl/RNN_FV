Tri Polyta ends at 6-7/8 in New York.

NEW YORK 1996-12-06

PT Tri Polyta Indonesia closed at 6-7/8 in New York on Friday. It recorded the day's high of 7-1/4 and the day's low 6-5/8.

Its previous close on Thursday was 7-1/2.

Volume on Friday was 45,700 American Depository Shares (ADS). Block volume was 15,000.

One ADS represents 10 common shares.
