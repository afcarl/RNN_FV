IMF combines Asian, Pacific departments.

WASHINGTON 1996-12-06

The International Monetary Fund said Friday it will combine its Central Asia, and Southeast Asia and Pacific departments to form the Asia and Pacific Department, effective Jan 1.

A regional office for Asia and the Pacific will be established at a location in Asia to be known in the near future, the IMF said.

Hubert Neiss, an Austrian national, will become will be director of the new department and Kunio Saito, a native of Japan, will be director of the new regional office, the IMF said.
