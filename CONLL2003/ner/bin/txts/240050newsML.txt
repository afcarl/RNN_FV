USDA seeks 554,400 lbs frozen pork for programs.

CHICAGO 1996-12-06

The Agricultural Marketing Service of the U.S. Department of Agriculture said it was seeking to buy 554,400 lbs of frozen fine ground pork, bulk pork sausage and pork sausage patties (3.0 ounce) for distribution to eligible outlets.

All offers must be in the format prescribed in LS-50 and be received by 1500 EST in Washington, D.C., on Friday, December 13. Acceptances will be announced by midnight Friday, December 20.

Shipment must be made between January 18 and February 10, 1997.
