Gruber & McBaine take 6.1 pct Gateway Data stake.

PHOENIX 1996-12-06

Gateway Data Science Corp said Gruber & McBaine Capital Management Inc. bought 171,600 shares -- or 6.1 percent -- of the company's common stock.

The company said San Francisco-based Gruber bought the shares as an investment.

The company did not say whether or not the purchase made Gruber Gateway Data's largest shareholder. Officials from Gateway Data were not immediately available to comment.
