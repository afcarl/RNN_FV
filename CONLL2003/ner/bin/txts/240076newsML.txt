Florida puts $44.445 mln GOs on 20-hour notice.

TALLAHASSEE, Fla 1996-12-06

Florida will sell $44.445 million of Department of Transportation general obligation bonds by competitive bid with 20 hours notice, the state Division of Bond Finance said Friday.

The bonds are Florida DOT Right-of-Way Acquisition and Bridge Construction Refunding Bonds, series 1996, and will refund the 1991 series. Maturities are out to 25 years.
