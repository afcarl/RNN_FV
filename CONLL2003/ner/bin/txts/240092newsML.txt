Municipal sales total $5.314 bln for week of Dec 9.

NEW YORK 1996-12-06

Scheduled competitive and negotiated municipal bond sales are expected to total $5.314 billion in 163 offerings for week of December 9, according to Reuters Municipal Bond Information Group.

Competitive sales total $1.384 billion in 94 issues. The largest issue is a $165 million Metro Atlanta RTA on Dec 11.

Negotiated sales total $3.930 billion in 69 offerings. The largest is a $579 million Municipal Assistance Corporation of New york City issue through Morgan Stanley Inc.

An additional $241 million of negotiated deals in eight offerings are on the possible sales calendar, meaning underwriters have said they may price them during the week depending on market conditions.
