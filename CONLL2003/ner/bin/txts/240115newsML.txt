Freddie Mac sets 4-for-1 stock split.

MCLEAN, Va. 1996-12-06

Federal Home Loan Mortgage Corp said Friday it authorized a 4-for-1 split of its common stock.

The stock distribution of three additional shares of voting common stock will be made to holders of record as of Dec 31, it said.
