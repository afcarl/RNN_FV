U.S. aluminum service center shipments off Jan-Oct.

NEW YORK 1996-12-06

U.S. aluminum service center shipments in the first ten months of the year were 1.4 percent below the same period in 1995, the National Association of Aluminum Distributors said.

According to NAAD's three-month moving average index, shipments in October rose to 139.7 points in October from 137.2 points in September. Inventory fell to 119.3 points from 121.2 over the same period.

Inventories still represent 2.7 months, unchanged from September.
