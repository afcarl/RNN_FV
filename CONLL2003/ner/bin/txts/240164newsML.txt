Micro Component launches chip system.

ST. PAUL, Minn. 1996-12-06

Micro Component Technology Inc said Friday it had introduced a new type of semiconductor manufacturing system as part of a project with Credence Systems Corp.

The so-called Centaur system workcell allows several steps in the computer chip manufacturing process to be executed together, said Micro Component.

The system also provides for complete integration of the tester, handler, manipulator, loadboard and contactor for chip testing devices.

Micro Component manufactures test-handling equipment for the semiconductor industry.
