U.S. chain store indicator off 0.1 pct Nov 30 - BTM.

NEW YORK 1996-12-06

The Bank of Tokyo-Mitsubishi/Chain Store Age weekly leading indicator of U.S. chain store sales fell 0.1 percent in the week ended November 30 from the prior week.

On a year-over-year basis, the index rose 0.3 percent in the survey week.

-- N.A. Treasury Desk, 212-859-1660
