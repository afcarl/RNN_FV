Muni dealers traded $1.08 bln bonds Thursday-MSRB.

NEW YORK 1996-12-06

Municipal bond dealers traded $1.08 billion of bonds Thursday on 3,795 trades, the Municipal Securities Rulemaking Board said.

A total of 2,356 individual issues changed hands, with 73 of those trading four or more times, MSRB said, citing figures from an automated clearance system of interdealer trades.

--U.S. Municipal Desk, 212-859-1650
