Central bank sees narrower Belgian, Lux c/a surplus.

BRUSSELS 1996-12-06

The Belgian central bank revised its forecast for the 1996 current account surplus of Belgium and Luxembourg to 5.1 percent of Gross Domestic Product (GDP), from a previous forecast of 5.7 percent made in August.

Belgium and Luxembourg are in an economic and currency union since March 1922.
