Italy offers 12 trln lire of BOTs for sale.

ROME 1996-12-06

The Italian Treasury said it was

offering 12.00 trillion lire of short term bills (BOT),

replacing 13.75 trillion lire of expiring paper.

A Treasury statement said it would offer:

*3.25 trillion lire of three-month BOTs

*4.25 trillion lire of six-month paper

*4.50 trillion lire of one-year bills.

Subscriptions close at 1200 GMT on December 10.

The Treasury added that it would offer 2.5 trillion lire of two-year zero couponed certificates (CTZ) with subscriptions for that sale also closing at 1200 GMT, December 10.
