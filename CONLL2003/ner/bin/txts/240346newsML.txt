Indian Sept steel output rises to 1.16 mln tonnes.

NEW DELHI 1996-12-06

Indian steel output rose to 1.16 million tonnes in September from 1.15 million in the same month in 1995, the government said on Friday.

    STEEL OUTPUT    Sept    Sept    Apr-Sept   Apr-Sept

Note - Figures are in thousands of tonnes and preliminary. They cover India's main plants.
