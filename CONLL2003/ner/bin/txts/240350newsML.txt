Indian Sept cement output rises to 5.4 mln tonnes.

NEW DELHI 1996-12-06

Indian cement production rose in September to 5.41 million tonnes from 5.19 million in the same month in 1995, the government said on Friday.

							   1996  1995

				5,411    5,190    37,248  33,198

Note - Figures are in thousands of tonnes and preliminary.
