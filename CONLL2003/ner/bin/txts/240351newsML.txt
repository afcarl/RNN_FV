Indian Sept coal output rises to 20.29 mln tonnes.

NEW DELHI 1996-12-06

Indian coal production rose in September to 20.29 million tonnes from 18.77 million in the same month in 1995, the government said on Friday.

    COAL PRODUCTION  Sept 96  Sept 95	Apr-Sept

						     1996     1995

			   20.29     18.77    120.62   111.99

Note - Figures are in million tonnes and preliminary.
