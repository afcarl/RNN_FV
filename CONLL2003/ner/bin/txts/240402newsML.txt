Indian Aug industrial output up 6.4 pct yr/yr.

NEW DELHI 1996-12-06

India's index of industrial production increased by 6.4 percent in August over the same month in 1995, a government official told Reuters on Friday.  

The official, who asked not to be named, said the index, which has 1980/81 (April-March) as its base year, stood at 292.9 in August.

He said the government planned to release the monthly statement on industrial production later on Friday.
