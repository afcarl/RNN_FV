Hatton National up on takeover news.

COLOMBO 1996-12-06

Hatton National Bank Ltd rose six percent, or 11 rupees to 251, on early turnover of 1,900 shares, dealers said.

"The price is rising due to news that HNB is going to buy the local branch of Indosuez," said a local broker.

HNB said on Friday it had reveived central bank permission to buy the local branch of Indosuez, and was doing a due diligence study.

HNB is one of four private commercial banks listed in Colombo.
