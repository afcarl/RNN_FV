Hatton National to buy Indosuez Colombo.

COLOMBO 1996-12-06

Sri Lankan blue chip Hatton National Bank Ltd said on Friday it had got Central Bank and Monetary Board permission to buy the Banque Indosuez bank branch in Colombo.

"We have got permission, and are now carrying out due diligence, which should be completed by Christmas," HNB managing director Rienzie Wijetilleke told Reuters.
