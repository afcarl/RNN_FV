Sallie Mae to price floating rate notes.

WASHINGTON 1996-12-06

The Student Loan Marketing Association said Friday it will price $500 million of short-term floating rate notes on Monday, December 9.

When-issued trading is expected to begin at 0820 EST/1320 GMT the following day, it said.

The notes are for settlement December 12. Maturity date is June 12, 1997. Cusip number is 86387TEX2.

Sallie Mae said the notes will be priced at a spread to the bond equivalent rate of the 91-day Treasury bill, with the interest rate reset the day after each weekly T-bill auction.
