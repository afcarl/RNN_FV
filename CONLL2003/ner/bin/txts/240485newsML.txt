Grant Geophysical files Chap 11 bankruptcy.

HOUSTON 1996-12-06

Grant Geophysical Inc on Friday said it filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy protection in the U.S. Bankruptcy Court in Delaware.

The company also said it anticipates the court will allow it principal lender, Foothill Capitol Corp, to provide additional funding for continuing operations. Grant expects to seek a purchaser for its domestic business "as soon as reasonably practicable," they said in a statement.

Grant also announced the resignation of president and chief executive officer Thomas Portwood Jr.

J. Kelly Elliott, currently chairman, was named acting president and chief executive.

About 40 employees at the company's Houston office were terminated as of December 2, as part of a cost-cutting measure, Grant added.
