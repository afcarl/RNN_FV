Equity sells 3.6 mln shares at $41.25/shr.

CHICAGO 1996-12-06

Equity Residential Properties Trust said on Friday it sold 3.6 million registered common shares of beneficial interest through Smith Barney Inc at a price of $41.25 per share.  

Equity said the sale will bring aggregate proceeds of $148.5 million and that it has granted Smith Barney a 30-day over-allotment option for an additional 540,000 shares.

The shares are included in the company's shelf registration that was declared effective by the Securities and Exchange Commission in September 1996, the company said.

The proceeds will be used to buy additional multifamily properties.

Equity Residential is a self-administered and managed equity real estate investment trust that owns 231 properties consisting of 68,712 units in 31 states.
