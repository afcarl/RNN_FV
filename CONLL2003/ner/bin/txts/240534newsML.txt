Fort Wayne National names chairman.

FORT WAYNE, Ind. 1996-12-06

Fort Wayne National Corp said on Friday that M. James Johnston will become chairman of the board and continue as chief executive officer, effective January 1.

He replaces Jackson Lehman, who as previously reported will retire on December 31, the company said.  

Stephen Gillig was named president and chief administrative officer at the company. He also serves as secretary of the board.

Karen Kasper was named chief financial officer and will continue as senior vice president and treasurer.

Thomas Elyea, who continues as senior vice president of the company, was named president and chief administrative officer of the company's unit Fort Wayne National Bank.
