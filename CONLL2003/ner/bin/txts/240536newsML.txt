TCSI ends talks with regional bell co.

BERKELEY, Calif. 1996-12-06

TCSI Corp, which provides software to the telecommunications industry, said on Friday that talks with a regional bell operating company for an anticipated contract have been terminated.

TCSI did not disclose the name of regional bell company.

TCSI said the deal would have generated significant on-going revenues.

It also it is in talks with the regional bell company for payment for work already performed.

TCSI said it continues to work with the company on another deal, without giving any details.
