EMERGENCY WEATHER CONDITIONS -  WSC - 06-DEC.

Tropical Cyclone 08B remains off the coast of Andhra Pradesh, India. Top winds are near 60 mph. This system is expected to weaken and dissipate rapidly while moving into Andhra Pradesh this period. Its leftover moisture will then progress into South Central India. Along the path of this system, heavy rains will result in flash flooding, which may be severe in some locations.  

Tropical Cyclone Daniella is a major threat to shipping in the South Indian Ocean. It is centered near 15s/55e, moving slowly west southwest. Top winds are near 130 mph and will remain steady or weaken slightly during the next 24 hours. It is still at least a few days away from Madagascar.
