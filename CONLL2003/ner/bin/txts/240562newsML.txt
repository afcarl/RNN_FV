Rainforest Cafe to develop Chicago site.

MINNEAPOLIS 1996-12-05

Rainforest Cafe Inc said Thursday it entered into a lease agreement to develop a restaurant in downtown Chicago.

Rainforest said it will build a 23,000 square foot free-standing cafe in downtown Chicago. The cafe is projected to open in the fall of 1997.
