EIB LENDS 33 MLN ECUS TO GREEK DEH.

ATHENS 1996-12-06

The European Investment Bank (EIB) lent 33 million ecus or 10 billion drachmas to Greece's Public Power Corporation (DEH), the EIB said in a statement.

Part of the loan, 26.4 million ecus, will go towards financing the construction of a fifth thermal unit in the electricity production station of DEH at Agios Dimitrios in Ptolemaida, Northern Greece.

The balance of 6.6 million ecus or 2.0 billion drachmas will finance the expansion and upgrading of the electricity distribution network in mainland Greece, it said. --Dimitris Kontogiannis, Athens Newsroom +301 3311812-4
