PHILIPS' SUPERCLUB MANAGER RESIGNS.

AMSTERDAM 1996-12-06

Philips Electronics NV on Friday said the general manager of its Superclub video rental business had resigned, effective January 1, 1997.

"Mr Fitters and Philips have decided by mutual agreement that (he) will resign as General Manager.. The decision follows differences of opinion about company policy," Philips said in a statement.

Superclub International B.V. is wholly owned by Philips and is part of its Software and Services sector, with stores principally in Benelux.
