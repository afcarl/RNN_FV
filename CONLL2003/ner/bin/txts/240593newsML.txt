Two Brent dates kept, two more to go.

LONDON 1996-12-06

Exxon kept two of the four Brent dates due to turn wet by five o'clock on Friday with two more still doing the paper rounds, traders said.

Four sets of dates were due to turn wet. Dated Brent was assessed at around Jan Brent +35/+40 cents with no new wet deals heard.
