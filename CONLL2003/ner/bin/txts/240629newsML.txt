C&W directors Solomon and Astle to leave firm.

LONDON 1996-12-06

Cable & Wireless Plc said on Friday that Jonathan Solomon, executive director strategy and business development, and Edward Astle, executive director of international services will be leaving the company.

Solomon will retire at the end of this year but will become a special adviser to the telecommunications giant. Astle will be leaving the company on March 31, 1997.
