Average discount rate at UK bill tender 6.0773 pct.

LONDON 1996-12-06

The average rate of discount at the weekly tender of 91-day UK Treasury bills fell to 6.0773 percent from 6.1328 percent last week, the Bank of England said on Friday.

The 200 million stg offer attracted bids of 905 million stg. The pro rata price was 98.48.

Next week 200 million stg of bills will be on offer.
