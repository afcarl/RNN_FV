Cazenove Indian GDR Index up 0.94 percent.

LONDON 1996-12-06

The Cazenove Indian GDR Index rose 0.57 point, or 0.94 percent, to 61.34 after the Bombay Stock Exchange 30 share index rose 13.98 points to 2,826.20.

The premium for GDRs over stocks on the local market was 11.66 percent.

The Cazenove index is a capitalisation weighted basket of Indian global depositary receipts and measures the change from midday on the previous trading day to midday today.
