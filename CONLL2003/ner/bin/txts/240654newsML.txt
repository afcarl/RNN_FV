NW Europe offshore oil, gas rigs fall four in week.

LONDON 1996-12-06

The number of active oil and gas rigs in operation offshore northwest Europe fell to 58 in the week to December 4, four down from the week earlier, consultants Arthur Andersen said in a report.

U.K. rigs fell one to 30, Norway dropped two to 16 while Dutch activity slipped one to eight. The Danish tally was steady at four rigs active.

Onshore U.K. drilling was down one rig at three.
