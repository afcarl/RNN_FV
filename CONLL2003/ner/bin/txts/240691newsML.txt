UK receiverships decline in November - survey.

LONDON 1996-12-06

The decline in UK receivership and administration order appointments continued in November, said accountant Deloitte & Touche.

It said in a statement that the 107 appointments recorded in November was the third lowest monthly figure for seven years.

The accountacy firm estimates that the total number of appointments for 1996 will be around 1600, which would represent a decline of 20 and 25 percent on 1995 and 1994 respectively.

London and the South East continued to have the largest number of appointments with 34 percent of the month's total.

But London and the South East also had the largest decrease with 36 appointments in November from 61 in October.
