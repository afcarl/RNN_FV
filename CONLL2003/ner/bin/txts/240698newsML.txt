UBS leads 795 million stg four-tranche bond.

LONDON 1996-12-06

Leek Finance No 1 Plc has issued a 794.6 million sterling four-tranche floating-rate bond, lead manager UBS said.

The bonds are backed by a pool of UK residential mortgages.
