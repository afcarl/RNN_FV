Prism confirmed for WAGN rail, GB for Anglia.

LONDON 1996-12-06

Britain's Office of Passenger Rail Franchising (Opraf) confirmed on Friday that Prism Rail Plc had been awarded the contract to run West Anglia/Great Northern (WAGN) train operating company, and GB Rail would be awarded the Anglia Rail contract.

Both groups had previously been identified as preferred bidders for the train operating companies which are among 25 such firms currently being privatised.

Details of the subsidy arrangements and any specific operating conditions will be announced at news briefings later this morning, Opraf said.
