Saudi beheads man for shooting Yemeni.

DUBAI 1996-12-06

Saudi Arabia beheaded a Saudi man on Friday for shooting dead a Yemeni man after a dispute, Saudi state-run television reported.

The beheading, in Jizan area in southwest Saudi Arabia, raised to 61 the number of people executed in the conservative Islamic kingdom so far this year.

Saudi Arabia strictly enforces Islamic sharia law by publicly beheading by the sword convicted murderers, drug smugglers, rapists and other criminals.
