MCA to buy 50 percent of Polish TV station.

UNIVERSAL CITY, Calif.

MCA Inc. said Thursday it reached an agreement to buy a 50 percent stake in Luxembourg-based media group CLT's RTL7, the free, satellite-and cable-delivered general entertainment local language channel in Poland.

RTL7 is scheduled to launch Dec. 7. Terms of the deal were not released.

In a statement, MCA said it will provide both first-run and library programming, and will create two Universal branded programming blocks, consisting of movie and action- adventure themes.

First-run titles will include, "Beethoven," "The Burning Zone," "Coach" and "EZ Streets," among others. Feature length titles will include "Airport," "The Land Before Time," "Dead Men Don't Wear Plaid," and more.
