New York certificated cotton stocks rise to 74,357.

NEW YORK 1996-12-06

Certificated cotton stocks deliverable against New York Cotton Exchange's No. 2 cotton futures contract as of Dec 5 totaled 74,357 bales, up from 73,719 bales the previous session.

There were 638 bales issued, 425 decertification orders, no cancellations, and 2,785 bales awaiting review.
