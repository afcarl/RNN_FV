CBOT overnight Project A ag volume 899 contracts.

CHICAGO 1996-12-06

Chicago Board of Trade said its Project A overnight agricultural volume totaled 899 contracts versus 288 overnight Wednesday.

There were 57 wheat, 221 corn, 489 soybean, 86 soymeal, 16 soyoil, four oat, 10 wheat call, four corn call, two soybean call and 10 soybean put contracts traded.

In overnight trading, wheat was steady to 1/4 cent higher, corn was 3/4 lower to 1/4 higher and soybeans were steady to two cents lower.
