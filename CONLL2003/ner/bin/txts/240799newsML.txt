Cyclone threatens ships in southern Indian Ocean.

LEXINGTON, Mass. 1996-12-06

Tropical Cyclone Daniella is a major threat to shipping in the southern Indian Ocean,, private forecaster Weather Services Corporation said on Friday.

It is centred near 15s/55e, moving slowly west southwest with top winds near 130 mph.

The storm will remain steady or weaken slightly during the next 24 hours.

It is still at least a few days away from Madagascar.
