UICI says in insurance acquisition talks.

DALLAS 1996-12-05

UICI, a financial services company, said on Thursday that it has entered into negotiations to acquire the insurance sales and marketing group of  United Group Association.

United Group's sales force consists of about 4,000 licensed agents who distribute insurance products underwritten by UICI's insurance company subsidiaries, it said.

United Group is 100 percent owned by UICI Chairman Ronald Jensen, who acquired it in 1983.
