Bel-20 shares end down but off lows.

1540 GMT - Brussels bourse closes down after Federal Reserve Chairman Alan Greenspan comments sparked global equity sell-off.

* Bel-20 share index  .BFX down 1.04 percent or 19.66 points to 1,871.46 after touching intraday low of 1,839.46. Trade heavy with over 3.8 billion worth of Belgian shares changing hands.

* Insurer Fortis AG fell 0.5 percent to 4,930 francs. Petrofina fell 3.04 percent to 10,375 francs with one dealer putting it down to its sensitivity toward the falling dollar.
