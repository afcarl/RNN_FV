MATIF says to open London office in February.

PARIS 1996-12-06

French futures and options exchange MATIF said on Friday it would open a representative office in London on February 1, 1997.

MATIF said in a statment, "This new marketing office will bolster MATIF's strategy for the move to a single currency."

MATIF said the office would be run by Jean-Francois Conil-Lacoste, who will be joining the exchange as director of marketing, a new position. He will be responsible for MATIF marketing as a whole and will be based in London.

Conil-Lacoste is currently head of capital markets at French brokerage Ferri SA in Paris.
