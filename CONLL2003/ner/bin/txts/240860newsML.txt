Paris airports' postal traffic rises Jan-Oct 96.

PARIS 1996-12-06

The Paris airports, Charles de Gaulle (CDG) and Orly, handled totalled 106,234 tonnes of postal traffic in the first ten months of 1996, an increase of 3.7 percent on the same month last year, Paris airport authority (ADP) figures show.
