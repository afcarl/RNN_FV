Peter Liessem leave DHL management.

FRANKFURT 1996-12-06

DHL Worldwide Express GbmH, the Germany division of DHL International, said Peter Liessem has left its management team.

His position would not be replaced due to re-organisation of the Germany management structure, a company statement said.

Liessem was DHL general manager for special projects and played a key role in building up the company in Germany during his 13 years with DHL, the statement added.
