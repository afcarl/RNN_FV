Gevaert ups IVG stake to 7.5%.

BRUSSELS 1996-12-06

Belgian holding company Gevaert NV said on Friday it had used a recent capital increase at German real estate group IVG Holding AG to raise its interest in the group to 7.5 percent from 2.0 percent.

Gevaert said it subscribed to the new shares, as well as bought shares on the open market, "at favourable conditions" but did not disclose terms.
