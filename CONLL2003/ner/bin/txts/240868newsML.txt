Union Miniere sells holding in TCI.

BRUSSELS 1996-12-06

Belgium's Union Miniere said on Friday it had sold its entire 10 percent holding in Thai Copper Industries and agreed to cancel all previous obligations and agreements with the company.  

"As of December 6, 1996 Union Miniere is no longer a shareholder of TCI," the company said in the statement.

"Existing shareholders of Thai Copper Industries have taken up UM's 10 percent stake in TCI," it added.

The company gave no financial details or reasons for the move.
