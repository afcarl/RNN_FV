French stocks slide on Wall Street drop.

PARIS 1996-12-06

French shares started a fresh slide after a deep Wall Street opening loss but traded aboved the day's lows, dealers said

The blue-chip CAC-40 index was down 78.06 points or 3.41 percent at 2,214.44 at 1447 GMT while the CAC-40 futures contract was down at 2,216 from 2,294 on Thursday's close.

The index had been as low as 2,180.44 in a sell-off prompted by remarks by U.S. Federal Reserve Chairman Alan Greenspan and exacerbated by statements by Bundesbank member Jochimsen. There is market talk Greenspan will make a statement today

* Volume was eight billion francs.
