Oresundsforbindelsen sets 200 mln Dkk 4-pct bond.

COPENHAGEN 1996-12-06

The following new bond issue

was announced on Friday by arranger Den Danske Bank.

BORROWER - A/S ORESUNDSFORBINDELSEN

AMT - 200 MILLION DANISH CROWNS

COUPON - FOUR PERCENT

MATURITY - 30 DECEMBER 2003

TYPE - SERIES LOAN

PAYMENTS - ANNUALLY ON DECEMBER 30 ISS

DATE - DECEMBER 20 1996

ISS PRICE - TO BE ADVISED

GUARANTOR - KINGDOM OF DENMARK
