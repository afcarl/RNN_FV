Air France adds France-Vietnam flight.

PARIS 1996-12-06

An increase in the Air France Airbus 340-300 passenger service between Paris and Hanoi, will mean extra 10 tonnes a week of cargo capacity on the route, a  Air France cargo official told Reuters.

The French national carrier is add a third weekly frequency to Hanoi, via Bangkok, effective December 15.

The A340-300's cargo capacity on the Paris-Hanoi route is around 10 tonnes per flight, he added.

Air France also operates a three times weekly A340-300 passenger service between Paris and Ho Chi Minh City via Bangkok.

Vietnam Airlines' operates three-weekly Boeing 767 passenger service between Paris and Ho Chi Minh City and upgraded its 767 service between Paris and Hanoi from one to two flights weekly, at end-October.

The 767 services to Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City offer cargo capacity of up to 70 tonnes per week, Vietnam Airlines's French cargo sales agent, Globe Air said. --Air Cargo Newsroom Tel+44 171 542 7706 Fax +44 171 542 5017 --
