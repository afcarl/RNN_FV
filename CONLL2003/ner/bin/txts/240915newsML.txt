Cartel office clears Thyssen's Witzig buy.

BONN 1996-12-06

Thyssen Produktionssysteme, a unit of the Thyssen group, said on Friday in a statement it had received approval from the German cartel office to take over Witzig & Frank Turmatic GmbH.

Thyssen gave no information on the purchase price, but said Witzig & Frank had 250 employees and annual turnover of 100 million marks ($65.3 million).

-- Bonn newsroom, 49-228-26097150 ($ = 1.531 German Marks)
