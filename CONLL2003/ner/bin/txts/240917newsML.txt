VA Tech gets Croatia order for 1.2 bln ATS.

VIENNA 1996-12-06

Austrian Energy, a unit of VA Technologie, said on Friday it had received an order from Croatia's Plomin d.o.o to deliver and construct a coal-fired power plant.

In a statement, VA Technologie said its unit was part of a consortium which includes Germany's Siemens and Croatia's Duro Dakovic TEP d.d. It said its share of the order was worth 1.2 billion schillings.
