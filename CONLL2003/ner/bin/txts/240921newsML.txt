Austria cenbank allots 11.69 bln ATS in repos.

VIENNA 1996-12-06

The Austrian central bank said on Friday it awarded 11.69 billion schillings in one-week securities repurchase agreements at a fixed rate of three percent.

The National Bank said it filled 12 percent of bids.

Last week, the bank allotted 25.26 billion schillings, or 26 percent of bids, in one-week repos, also at a fixed rate of three percent. -- Vienna Newsroom, 43-1-53112-258
