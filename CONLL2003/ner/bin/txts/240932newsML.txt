Buyout bid for France's Au Bon Marche, Fin Agache.

PARIS 1996-12-06

Montaigne Participations et Gestions, a holding of Groupe Arnault, said on Friday it made an offer to buy out minority shareholders of retailer Au Bon Marche and Financiere Agache.

The offer price for Financiere Agache was 2,317 francs per share and the offer price for Au Bon Marche was be 1,596 francs, Montaigne Participations et Gestions said in a statement.
