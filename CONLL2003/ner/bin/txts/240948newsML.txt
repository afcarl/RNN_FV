Irish live register 268,700 s.a. in November - CSO.

DUBLIN 1996-12-06

The numbers unemployed as measured by the live register showed a seasonally adjusted 268,700, Central Statistics Office (CSO) figures showed on Friday.  

The live register, which shows the number of people claiming unemployment benefits, fell to an unadjusted 263,305 versus 267,586 the previous month.

			  Unadjusted	    Adjusted

 Latest		  263,305		 268,700

 Previous		267,586		 273,800

 Year-ago		274,705		 280,200
