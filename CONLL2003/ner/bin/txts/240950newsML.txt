EU approves Thai broken rice quota.

BRUSSELS 1996-12-06

The European Union's rice management committee on Thursday approved a new tariff-free quota for Thai broken rice, an EU official said on Friday.

"The committee voted the quota for 12,500 tonnes of broken rice, which is a GATT (General Agreement on Tariffs and Trade) commitment," the official said.
