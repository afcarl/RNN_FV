Swedish Nov jobless up to 7.9 pct vs Oct's 7.6 pct.

STOCKHOLM 1996-12-06

Unemployment in Sweden rose to 7.9 percent in November from 7.6 percent in October, Statistics Sweden said on Friday.

  SWEDISH UNEMPLOYMENT	 Nov 96	Oct 96     Nov 95

  Percent of workforce	 7.9	   7.6	  7.2

  Number of unemployed	 336,000     326,000    309,000

Note - A Reuter poll of economists showed the market had expected an unemployment rate of 7.4 percent in November.
