PRESS DIGEST - France - Le Monde Dec 6.

PARIS 1996-12-06

These are leading stories in afternoon daily Le Monde.

FRONT PAGE

-- Wife of Paris mayor, Xaviere Tiberi, placed under legal investigation for alleged misuse of public funds.

BUSINESS PAGES

-- Budget Minister Alain Lamassoure confirms a planned 11-billion-franc Thomson recapitalisation that includes 9.9 billion for Thomson Multimedia, plus plans to buy back from Thomson its stake in Credit Lyonnais for 2.9 billion. Government does not rule out breaking up Thomson, ahead of privatisation.

-- Privatisation Commission's decision to deny approval of planned sale of Thomson to Lagardere, which included sale of Thomson Multimedia to Korea's Daewoo, hits French image in Asia.

-- Blue-chip CAC 40 index opens down 2.44 percent after U.S. Federal Reserve chairman Alan Greenspan warned against "irrational exuberance" on the stock markets.

-- Welfare debt repayment fund CADES' five-year, $2 billion bond issue met favourably by international investors.
