Denmark's Hypotekbanken raises four-pct bond.

COPENHAGEN 1996-12-05

Denmark's Hypotekbanken on Friday raised an existing 1.4 billion crown, four-percent, six-year bond offer to 1.5 billion crowns, lead manager Gudme Raaschou Bankaktieselskab said in a statement distributed by the Copenhagen Stock Exchange.

The statement said the new bonds, due to mature in 2002, were sold.
