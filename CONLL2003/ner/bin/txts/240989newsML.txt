German cocoa imports sharply higher in August.

WIESBADEN, Germany 1996-12-06

German January-August 1996 cocoa bean imports from third countries at 202,116 tonnes exceeded the year-earlier level of 174,338 after August imports were sharply higher, official data showed on Friday.

Imports in August were 44,379 tonnes after 12,688 a year earlier and compared with July imports of 22,701 tonnes.

August import value was 98.76 million marks against 29.21 million a year earlier.

Leading suppliers in the eight months were Ivory Coast with 118,284 tonnes, Ghana with 32,276, Indonesia with 19,783 and Nigeria with 11,322, excluding beans for toll grinding.
