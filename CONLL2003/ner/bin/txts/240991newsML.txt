Ericsson wins 200 mln SKR S.Korea order.

STOCKHOLM 1996-12-06

Swedish telecommunications group LM Ericsson AB said on Friday it won AXE switching orders worth 200 million crowns from Korea Telecom and ONSE Telecom in South Korea.

Ericsson will deliver AXE switches and Intelligent Network (IN) solutions to most fixed networks, the company said.

For Korea Telecom, Ericsson will install AXE equipment in the city of Taejon.

For ONSE Telecom, Ericsson will deliver international AXE switching equipment with IN solutions, an access switch and an operational and supervising system.
