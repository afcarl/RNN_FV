HSBC slides after Far East sell-off.

LONDON 1996-12-06

Shares in HSBC Holdings were sharply lower on Friday, tracking heavy losses in the Far East markets overnight.

The shares were 38p lower at 12.85-1/2 stg by 0853 GMT.

In Hong Kong the Hang Seng index fell 2.88 percent or 388 points as remarks made by Fed chairman Alan Greenspan sparked renewed interest rate jitters and a sell-off in U.S bonds.

In Tokyo, the Nikkei 225 fell 3.19 percent, losing 667.20 points.
