Greenalls sells U.S. hotels for 14 mln stg.

LONDON 1996-12-06

Greenalls Group Plc said on Friday that it has sold its six hotels in the U.S. to the Shaner Hotel Group for 14.0 million stg in cash.

The UK pubs to off-licences group said in a statement that the book value of the hotels is 13.5 million and, after allowing for costs of disposal and settlement of contractual arrangements, the loss on disposal is 1.75 million.

Andrew Thomas, chairman and chief executive of Greenalls, said, "It has long been our intention to sell these hotels as they do not form part of our core business."
