Ashquay has 31.8 percent of UK Estates.

LONDON 1996-12-06

Ashquay Group Plc said on Friday that it has received acceptances representing 28 percent for its offer for UK Estates Plc by December 5.

The property company also said it has agreed to acquire 2.17 million ordinary shares, 3.8 percent of Ashquay's share capital.

Ashquay launched a bid for the British property development firm in November, offering 10 shares for every 13 UK Estate ordinary shares, vauling the target company at 21.1 million stg.

UK Estates has told shareholders to take no action.
