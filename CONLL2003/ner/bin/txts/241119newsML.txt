RTRS-NAB raises benchmark rate to 9.90 pct.

MELBOURNE 1996-12-06

National Australia Bank Ltd said on Friday it would increase its benchmark (prime) lending rate to 9.90 percent from 9.85 percent effective Monday.  

The rate applies to major corporate borrowers and varies with movements in the cost of short-term funding, the bank said.
