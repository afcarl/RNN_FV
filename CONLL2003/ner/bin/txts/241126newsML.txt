RTRS-Herald sells bulk of Lynas stake.

PERTH 1996-12-06

Gold producer Herald Resources Ltd said on Friday it had ceased to be a substantial shareholder in Perth-based Lynas Gold NL.

Herald said it had sold 29.46 million ordinary shares and 5.82 million June 1999 options today for a total consideration of A$5.84 million.

Herald said it retained a holding 5.0 million June 1999 options.
