RTRS-Westpac cuts rates on fixed rate loans.

SYDNEY 1996-12-06

Westpac Banking Corp said on Friday it will the lower interest rates on its two- and four-year fixed rate housing and development loans.

The cuts will take effect from December 9.

Rates for home loans and investment property loans were cut by 0.15 percentage points to 8.20 percent for a two-year loan and 8.60 percent for a four-year loan.

Rates for fixed rate business development loans were also cut by 0.15 percentage points to 8.45 percent for a two-year loan and 8.85 percent for a four-year loan.
