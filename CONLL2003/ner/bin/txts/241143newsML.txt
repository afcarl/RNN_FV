Underground to place 1.26 mln shares.

WELLINGTON 1996-12-06

Fashion retailer Underground Fashions Ltd said on Friday it was considering a placement of 1.26 million shares at NZ$0.20 per share.

The offer is open to clients of D F Mainland Securities Ltd, and has an attaching one-for-one option to acquire a further share in 12 months at NZ$0.25 per share.

The company said the proceeds of the issue would be used for working capital.  

Underground said it was confident of returning to profitability in the second half of the current year and was pleased with the confidence shown in the company through this issue.

Stuart Cairns, a director of D F Mainland & Co, has been appointed to Underground's board.
