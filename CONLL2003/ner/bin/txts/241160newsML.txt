RTRS-Australia to tender A$300 mln 2000 TAB bond.

SYDNEY 1996-12-06

The Treasury will offer A$300 million of October 2000 Treasury adjustable rate bonds (TABs) at a tender on Monday, the Reserve Bank of Australia said.  

The RBA said bids would be received until 12.45 pm. (0145 GMT) on Monday and results will be announced at 2.00 p.m. (0300 GMT). Settlement date is December 11.
