South Korea's 3-yr yields hit new year high.

Yields on three-year benchmark corporate bonds rose on Friday to close at a new year high of 12.65 percent amid depressed buying sentiment, brokers said.

This is the highest since September 27, 1995, they said.

* 3-yr corporates close at 12.65 vs 12.59 pct

* 3-mth CDs end unchanged at 13.70

* Overnight average call rates unchanged at 13.00
