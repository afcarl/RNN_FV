PRESS DIGEST - Chinese newspapers - Dec 6.

Following is a summary of major Chinese political and business stories in leading newspapers, prepared by Reuters in Beijing. Reuters has not checked the stories and does not guarantee their accuracy. Telephone: (8610) 6532-1921. Fax: (8610) 6532-4978.

- - - -

PEOPLE'S DAILY

Premier Li Peng urges visiting U.S. Republican congressmen to follow in the steps of their forerunners in the 1970s and make continuous efforts to promote Sino-U.S. relations.

Tian Jiyun, vice-chairman of the National People's Congress, or parliament, vows to crack down on lawless officials by stepping up supervision of the executive and judicial branches of government.

- - - -

CHINA DAILY

A commentary hails President Jiang Zemin's tour of the Philippines, India, Pakistan and Nepal as a diplomatic success that promotes stable and friendly relations between China and those nations.

- - - -

CHINA SECURITIES

The China Securities Regulatory Commission punishes 28 firms for illegal activities in share issues, including many subsidiaries of the country's main state banks.

- - - -

SECURITIES DAILY

China's stock accounts numbered more than 20 million at the end of November.
