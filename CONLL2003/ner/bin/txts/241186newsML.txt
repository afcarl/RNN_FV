KOMPAS

Customs officials in central Java detected a packet with 108,290 pills of the designer drug Ecstasy as it arrived through the country's postal system from Hong Kong.

JAKARTA POST

Indonesian businessmen continue to ignore environmental protection measures, Environment Minister Sarwono Kusumaatmadja said.

- - - -

REPUBLIKA

President Suharto inspected the Jalesveva Jayamahe statue at the Surabaya naval base. The statue, which symbolises an ideal marine, cost 26 billion rupiah to manufacture and used 360 tonnes of copper.

- - - -

MEDIA INDONESIA

The head of the armed forces' social and political affairs unit, Lieutenant General Syarwan Hamid, said that the country's armed forces (ABRI) were not worried about who would succeed President Suharto as Indonesia's next head of state. Hamid said there were plenty of other well qualified potential leaders.
