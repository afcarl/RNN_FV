RESEARCH ALERT - Indosat upped to buy.

-- Deutsche Morgan Grenfell said on Thursday it had raised its rating for PT Indonesian Satellite Corp, or Indosat, to buy from hold.

-- "The stock has declined 10 percent over the past week, owing to the reduction in accounting settlement rates in the United States," analyst Viktor Shvets wrote in a report.

-- The firm said the stock should trade in a range between $30 and $32.

-- "We are cautious on Indosat's long-term story, but believe that the stock has scope to appreciate 10-15 percent in the near term," Shvets wrote.

-- The company's stock was unchanged at 27 in New York.
