Sony Corp shares race higher in morning.

TOKYO 1996-12-06

Sony Corp shares raced to a new high for the year in Friday morning trade as domestic institutional investors joined in buying of the bellwether issue, brokers said.

At 0103 GMT, the shares were up 80 yen to 7,580 yen.  

Brokers said the market was focusing on the shares and whether or not they would have the strength to lead the market as a whole.
