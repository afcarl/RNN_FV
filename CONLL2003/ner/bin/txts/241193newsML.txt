Ricoh Co Ltd rises in early morning trade.

TOKYO 1996-12-06

Shares in Ricoh Co Ltd continued to rise in early morning Friday trade after they hit a high for the year on Thursday, bolstered by the market's quest for blue-chip issues, brokers said.

As of 0041 GMT, the shares were up 10 yen at 1,320 yen, the most heavily traded issue on the first section of the Tokyo Stock Exchange in volume of 3.10 million shares.
