Thai interbank rate eases, baht lower late.

BANGKOK 1996-12-06

The Thai overnight interbank rate fell to 9.00-9.50 percent in late trade on Friday from 10.00-10.50 percent earlier on higher liquidity after the passing of central bank's reserve requirement, dealers said.

"Banks were seen offering their excess funds to the market in the afternoon after they maintained sufficient funds to meet the reserve requirement," said one dealer at a foreign bank.

All commercial bank have to meet the Bank of Thailand's reserve requirement on seventh and 22nd of each month.
