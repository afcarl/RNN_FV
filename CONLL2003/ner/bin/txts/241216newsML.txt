Fuji Bank sets preferred stock issue terms.

TOKYO 1996-12-06

Fuji Bank Ltd said on Friday it had set the conversion price of its 70 million share 210 billion yen preferred stock issue at 2,002 yen.

The price is 1.1 times higher than the bank's closing share price of 1,820 yen on Friday, it said.

The payment date is December 27.
