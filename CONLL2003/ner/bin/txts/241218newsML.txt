USTR Barshefsky to hold briefing in Tokyo on Dec 7.

TOKYO 1996-12-06

Acting U.S. Trade Representative Charlene Barshefsky will hold a news conference in Tokyo on December 7 from 2 p.m. (0500 GMT), a U.S. embassy statement said on Friday.

Barshefsky and Japanese Finance Minister Hiroshi Mitsuzuka are scheduled to start ministerial talks later on Friday in an effort to resolve by a December 15 deadline a bilateral dispute over how to deregulate Japan's insurance market.
