Japan temperatures seen average for Dec 7 - Jan 6.

TOKYO 1996-12-06

Temperatures and rainfall are forecast to be about average during the month from December 7 to January 6, the Meteorological Agency said on Friday.

During the first week, from December 7 to December 13, temperatures in northern and eastern Japan are likely to be higher than average, it said.

Temperatures in other regions are expected to be average for the season, it said.

For the rest of the month, temperatures throughout Japan are expected to be about average, it said.
