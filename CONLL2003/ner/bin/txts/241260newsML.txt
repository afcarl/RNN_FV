Yuken Kogyo says to merge with sales subsidiary.

TOKYO 1996-12-06

Japanese hydraulic equipment maker Yuken Kogyo Co Ltd said on Friday it would merge with its sales subsidiary Yuken Himex KK next April.

It said Yuken Himex would be dissolved in the merger and each of its shares exchanged for one Yuken Kogyo share.

As a result of the merger, Yuken Kogyo's total assets would increase by 2.7 billion yen to 27.5 billion yen, a company statement said.

The company said it expects its parent current profit to rise to 983 million yen in the 1997/98 business year, up 56 million yen from the estimate for the current year.
