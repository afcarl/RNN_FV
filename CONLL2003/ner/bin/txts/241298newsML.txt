Japan realtor and affiliate declared bankrupt.

TOKYO 1996-12-06

The Wakayama District Court said on Friday that a local realtor and its finance affiliate were declared bankrupt on December 5.

The names of the two companies were translated as Kansai Sogo Jutaku and Kansai Finance.

A court spokesman said it could not provide any more details, including their total liabilities.

Private credit research firm Teikoku Databank Ltd said the two companies had total debts of 82.05 billion yen.
