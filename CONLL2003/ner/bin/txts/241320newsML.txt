Alsons plans new cement terminal.

MANILA 1996-12-06

Alsons Cement Corp (Alcem) said on Friday it was planning a new cement terminal in Batangas, just south of Manila.

Alcem assistant corporate secretary Allen Morallos told the Philippine Stock Exchange the management had got board approval to "negotiate ... for the procurement of permits for the construction of its cement terminal in Calaca, Batangas."

Alcem is based in the southern Philippines.
