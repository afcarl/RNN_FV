Omico starts investment house operations.

MANILA 1996-12-06

Mining firm Omico Corp said on Friday it had refocused the operation of its subsidiary to investment banking.

In a statement to the Philippine Stock Exchange, Omico said it had changed the name of Omico Resources Corp, a holding company, into Omico Kapital Corp, an investment house.

The amendments have been approved by the Securities and Exchange Commission.

The investment house will underwrite bonds and securities.
