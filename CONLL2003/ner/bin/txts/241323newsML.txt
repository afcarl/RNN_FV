Japan rubber stocks fall at end-Nov from Oct.

TOKYO 1996-12-06

Japan rubber stock figures at the end of November, according to the Japan Rubber Trade Association. Month-earlier and year-earlier comparisons, in tonnes, were as follows:

    Month	    Nov 96   Oct 96   Nov 95

    Stocks	   45,741   53,428   30,337

Breakdown of stocks, in tonnes

    Crude rubber   39,698   47,182   23,602

    Synthetic	 5,932    6,153    6,655

    Latex		 111	 93	 80
