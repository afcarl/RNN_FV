Philippine firms plan to buy 35,000 T U.S. wheat.

MANILA 1996-12-06

Three Philippine firms have invited tenders for a total 35,000 tonnes of U.S. wheat for December 16 to January 31 shipment, traders said on Friday.

The three firms asked for price quotations for 28,750 tonnes dark northern spring wheat and 6,250 tonnes of western white wheat, they said.

"The bids will be opened at 3 p.m. (0700 GMT) today," one trader said.
