Japan November ethylene output rises 2.5 pct yr/yr.

TOKYO 1996-12-06

Japan's ethylene production rose by 2.5 percent in November from a year earlier to 583,000 tonnes due to vigorous domestic and overseas demand, a trade ministry official said on Friday.

Japan's total ethylene output in calendar 1996 is expected to surpass seven million tonnes for the first time, he said.

Cumulative output from January to November added up to 6.47 million tonnes, he said.

The previous record high for annual ethylene output was in 1995, when production hit 6.94 million tonnes, he said.
