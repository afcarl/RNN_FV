Metroplex seeks continued suspension.

KUALA LUMPUR 1996-12-06

Metroplex Bhd said it is seeking a continued trading suspension in its stock pending an announcement.

In a statement sent to the Kuala Lumpur Stock Exchange late on Thursday, the company said the suspension is "pending another unrelated formal announcement". It did not offer details and a company representative declined further comment.

Shares of Metroplex closed at 3.30 ringgit on December 4.

On Thursday, the company said it plans to focus on hotel and leisure activities under a proposed restructuring plan, which will also involve the disposal of its property development subsidiaries.

-- Kuala Lumpur newsroom (603 230 8911)
