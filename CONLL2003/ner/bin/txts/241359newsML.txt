Over 700 die on Shanghai roads in first 11 months.

SHANGHAI 1996-12-06

A total of 715 people were killed in road accidents in Shanghai in the first 11 months of 1996, a number similar to last year's, the Business News said on Friday.

The newspaper said there were 18,295 traffic accidents in the city between January and November, against 16,728 for the whole of 1995.

The death rate remained largely static, with 788 people killed in the whole of 1995. The newspaper gave no comparison figures for the January-November period.
