Turner Broadcasting names Asia Pacific chief.

HONG KONG 1996-12-06

Turner Broadcasting System Inc (TBS) said on Friday it named Michael Byrd managing director, Turner Broadcasting System Asia Pacific Inc, effective immediately.

In the newly created position, Byrd will continue to be based in Hong Kong and will oversess all sales distribution and marketing for TBS in the Asia Pacific region, including network development, programming, marketing and promotion, it said.

Byrd was named president of Turner International Asia Pacific in May, 1996 and has been based in Hong Kong since September.

TBS, a subsidiary of Time Warner Inc, is a supplier of programming for the cable industry and a distributor of news and entertaniment product around the world.
