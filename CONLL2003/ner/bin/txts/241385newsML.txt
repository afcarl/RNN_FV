Shanghai Friendship to issue up to 50 mln B share.

SHANGHAI 1996-12-06

China's Shanghai Friendship & Overseas Chinese said in a statement on Friday it plans to issue up to 50 million new B shares.

The issue was approved by a company shareholders' meeting last week but has yet to be approved by the securities authorities, it said.

The price and timing of the issue has not yet been decided, it added. It gave no more details.

Shanghai Friendship & Overseas Chinese currently has a total share capital of 112.2 million shares, including 52.8 million B shares, 13.2 million A shares, 6.6 million shares hold by institutions and 39.6 million state shares.
