Dong-A Pharm says buying back shares.

SEOUL 1996-12-06

South Korea's Dong-A Pharmaceutical said in a statement on Friday it would buy back 50,000 of its own common shares on the open market to boost share prices.

The buyback, representing 0.61 percent of its share capital, will take place between December 9 and March 8, a company statement said.  

Dong-A currently holds 24,020 of its own common shares, which represents 0.29 percent of its share capital.

The statement said the purchases would be made at the closing price of the previous day.

Dong-A shares were trading at 18,000 won on Friday morning, unchanged from Thursday.
