PCIBank unit's IPO priced at 6.95 pesos.

MANILA 1996-12-06

PCI Leasing and Finance Inc said on Friday it had set the price for its initial public offering at 6.95 pesos per share.

The leasing company is selling 121 million shares, which is 20 percent of the company's total equity shares, the company said.

Offering period starts on December 11 and ends on December 20. Listing is scheduled on January 6.

PCI Leasing is a subsidiary of Philippine Commercial International Bank (PCIBank), one of the country's biggest banks. - Manila newsroom 63 2 8418938 Fax8176267
