Malaysian Hai-O debuts at 10 rgt.

KUALA LUMPUR 1996-12-06

Malaysian pharmaceuticals manufacturer Hai-O Enterpirse Bhd debuted on the Kuala Lumpur Stock Exchange at 10.00 ringgit against an offer price of 3.00 ringgit per share.

The company is listed on the Second Baord.  

The company offered 4.07 million shares to the public.

Jupiter Securities said in a morning report the fair trading range for the stock was between 9.50 and 10.60 ringgit.

Hai-O is a vendor and distributor of medicinal products and also manufactures traditional medicines and healthcare products.The company also has a chain of 29 retail outlets for its products.
