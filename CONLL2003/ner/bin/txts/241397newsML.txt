Fuji Photo jumps to new high for the year.

TOKYO 1996-12-06

Shares of Fuji Photo Film Co Ltd jumped to a new high for the year on Friday morning as investors bought the issue for their portfolios, brokers said.

At 0113 GMT, the shares were up 160 yen to 3,910 yen, the eighth most heavily traded issue on the first section of the Tokyo Stock Exchange in a trade of 2.182 million shares.
