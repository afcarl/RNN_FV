Bank Indonesia supplies 468 bln rph temporarily.

JAKARTA 1996-12-06

Indonesia's central bank, Bank Indonesia, supplied some 468 billion rupiah temporarily on the money market early on Friday, money traders said.

Some 777 billion rupiah of central bank certificates (SBIs) were due today, compared with some 259 billion rupiah of maturing short-term papers (SBPUs) and 50 billion rupiah of SBIs to be settled today.
