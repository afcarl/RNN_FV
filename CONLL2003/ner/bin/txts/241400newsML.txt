NTT to bid for stake in Brazil phone firm - paper.

TOKYO 1996-12-06

Nippon Telegraph and Telephone Corp (NTT) will form a consortium to bid for a 35-percent stake in a Brazilian regional telephone company, business daily Nihon Keizai Shimbun reported on Friday.

NTT will form a consortium comprised of Japanese trading firm Itochu Corp and local Brazilian companies, and will offer 50 to 60 billion yen for a 35 percent stake in the phone company, the paper said.  

An NTT spokeswoman said she was unable to confirm the report immediately.
