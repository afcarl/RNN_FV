Pacific Plywood says plans share split.

HONG KONG 1996-12-06

Hong Kong's Pacific Plywood Holdings Ltd said on Friday it planned to split each of its shares into four to increase the stock's liquidity.

Shareholders will be asked to approve the proposal at a special general meeting to be held on January 2, the company said in a statement.

Pacific Plywood said 968 million shares were currently in issue. The number would rise to 3.87 billion after the split.

The stock closed at HK$5.90 on Thursday.
