Korea Gas launches 2.0% samurai bonds--Nomura Sec.

TOKYO 1996-12-06

Korea Gas Corp has launched 10 billion yen worth of 2.0 percent samurai bonds due for December 2001, lead manager Nomura Securities said on Friday.
