German feed pea crop rises to 291,000 tonnes.

BONN 1996-12-06

Output of German feed peas, a compound feed ingredient, rose to 291,000 tonnes in 1996 from 216,400 tonnes in 1995 due to increases in the eastern German planted area, the Bonn farm ministry said on Friday.

National plantings were raised to 86,651 hectares from 64,195 ha in 1995, of which 67,219 ha were in eastern Germany (44,574 in 1995).

National yields were broadly steady at 3.36 tonnes per hectare versus 3.37 last year.
