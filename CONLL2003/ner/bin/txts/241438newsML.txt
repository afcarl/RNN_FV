Bank of Portugal injects 7.0 bln esc, 6.9 pct.

LISBON 1996-12-06

The Bank of Portugal said it injected seven billion escudos of six-day funds into the money market at an unchanged weighted average rate of 6.9 percent in a variable rate repurchase tender.

It offered 40 billion escudos in today's repo.
