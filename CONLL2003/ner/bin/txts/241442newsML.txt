Spanish markets closed for public holiday.

MADRID 1996-12-06

Spain's financial markets were closed for the Constitution Day public holiday on Friday. They will reopen on Monday.

Closing levels for bonds and the peseta on Thursday were as follows:

* 10-yr 8.80 pct T-bond 111.32 vs 112.19, yield 7.09 vs 6.97

* Dec bond future 111.37 vs 112.08 at yesterday's close

* Peseta/mark 84.18/20 at 1615 GMT vs 84.14/16
