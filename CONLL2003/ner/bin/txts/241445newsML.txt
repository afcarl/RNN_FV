Greek Commercial bank dealers on stop work.

ATHENS 1996-12-06

The dealers of state Commercial Bank of Greece went on a work stoppage between 1000 and 1300 local time (0800 and 1100 GMT) to demand equal pay with their colleagues at other state banks, bank employees told Reuters.

"The dealing is closed," a Commercial Bank employee told Reuters. "The dealers are on strike demanding that their pay be equal with that of their colleagues at state National Bank and other state banks."

--Dimitris Kontogiannis, Athens Newsroom +301 3311812-4
