PRESS DIGEST - Malta - Dec 6.

VALLETTA 1996-12-06

The following are the leading stories in the Maltese press on Friday. Reuters has not verified these stories and does not vouch for their accuracy.

THE TIMES

- President Bonnici, at opening of parliament, says new Labour government will seek to stabilise and better control government spending, curb tax evasion, invest in education and health, replace value-added tax with system of indirect taxation.

L-ORIZZONT

- President tells parliament that government has inherited serious financial problems after lack of control on spending under previous administration. No details given.

IN-NAZZJON

- Myriam Spiteri Debono, former Labour Party candidate, appointed speaker of parliament in agreement between government and opposition; first woman to hold position in Malta.
