Swiss launch six-month money market claims.

ZURICH 1996-12-06

The Swiss federal government is issuing six-month money market claims to be paid for on December 12, the Swiss National Bank said.

The allocation follows the end of subscriptions on December 10.

The previous issue of six-month claims, paid for on October 31, raised 919.00 million francs and was priced at 99.168 percent to yield 1.660 percent annually.
