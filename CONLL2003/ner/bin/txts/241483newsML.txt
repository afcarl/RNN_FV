Swiss short-time work rose in October.

BERNE 1996-12-06

The number of people affected by short-term working in Switzerland rose to 12,332 in October, up 2,982 from the previous month, the Federal Office for Industry, Trade and Labour said.

The number of work hours lost to short-time working rose by 230,122 to 694,692. The number of companies affected was 169 higher at 919.

In October last year, 501,467 hours were lost, affecting 9,321 workers in 906 firms.
