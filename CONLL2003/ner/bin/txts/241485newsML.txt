Swiss November unemployment rate rises to 5.1 pct.

BERNE 1996-12-06

Switzerland's unemployment rate stood at 5.1 percent in November, up from 4.8 percent the month before, the Federal Office of Trade, Industry and Labour said.

					  Nov 1996   Oct 1996   Nov 1995

 Pct of workforce unemployed    5.1	  4.8	  4.2

 Number of jobless		  183,026    174,107    152,102

 Job vacancies			5,218	5,344	4,535
