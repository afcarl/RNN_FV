RUGBY UNION-SOUTH AFRICA BEAT FRANCE 13-12.

PARIS 1996-12-07

South Africa beat France 13-12 (half-time 10-3) in a rugby union international on Saturday.

Scorers:

France - Penalties: Richard Dourthe (4)

South Africa - Try: James Dalton. Conversion: Henry Honiball. Penalties: Honiball (2)

Attendance : 45,000
