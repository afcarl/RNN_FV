SQUASH-JANSHER ASKED TO EXPLAIN ABSENCE.

BOMBAY 1996-12-09

World Open champion Jansher Khan has been asked to explain within 10 days his late withdrawal from the Mahindra International which finished here on Saturday.

If Jansher is considered to have contravened Professional Squash Association regulations he is liable to a fine of 10,000 pounds sterling ($16,400) plus a ban of between one month and a year.
