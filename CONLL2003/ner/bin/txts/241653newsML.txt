Greek wheat vessel runs aground in River Plate.

LONDON 1996-12-07

The Greek bulk carrier Anangel Wisdom ran aground near buoy 6 in the Punta Indio channel in the River Plate on December 6, Lloyds Shipping Intelligence Service reported.

The 22,353 dwt Anangel Wisdom, bound for Chile with a cargo of wheat, was proceeding downriver, Lloyds said without giving further details.
