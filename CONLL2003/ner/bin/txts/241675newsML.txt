PRESS DIGEST - Nigeria - Dec 7.

LAGOS 1996-12-07

These are significant stories in the Nigerian press on Saturday. Reuters has not verified these stories and does not vouch for their accuracy.

WEEKEND TIMES

- Government may ban the importation of very old, used aeroplanes in the same way as it has banned the importation of old cars. This follows the crash last month of a Boeing 727 airliner in which 142 people were killed.

THE GUARDIAN

- Panel probes crash of Aviation Development Company Boeing 727 last month.

- Shell will this year spend 16 billion naira on community assistance programmes.

THISDAY

- The army may on Friday have approved promotions for 240 officers and 5,410 other ranks.

($1=80 naira)
