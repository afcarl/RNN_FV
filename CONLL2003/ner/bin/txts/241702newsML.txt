Colombia to open bidding for coal fields Jan. 15.

BOGOTA 1996-12-06

Colombia will open the bidding process for rights to explore and operate five coal fields in the northernmost part of the country on January 15, Mines and Energy Minister Rodrigo Villamizar said.

He said the fields include El Descanso in Cesar province, which boasts an estimated 926 million metric tons of coal and El Hatillo, also in Cesar, which has estimated reserves of 71 million metric tons.

The others include Cesarito, a sprawling field in Cesar comprising more than 850,000 acres, and Cerrejon Sur in Guajira.

-- Juan Guillermo Londono, Bogota newsroom, 571 610 7944
