Malaysia PNB reduces stake in Golden Hope.

KUALA LUMPUR 1996-12-07

Malaysia's state equities investor Permodalan Nasional Bhd (PNB) has reduced its stake in Golden Hope Plantations Bhd by 5.98 percent, a Golden Hope statement said on Saturday.

It said PNB sold 60 million Golden Hope shares in two separate divestments in September and has since held 51.94 million shares or a 5.78 percent stake in the company.

PNB's previous shareholding in Golden Hope was 11.76 percent. -- Kuala Lumpur newsroom (603-2307509)
