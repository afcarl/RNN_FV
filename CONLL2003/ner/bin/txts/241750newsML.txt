Bank BDNI announces new rights issue.

JAKARTA 1996-12-07

PT Bank Dagang Nasional Indonesia (BDNI) will issue 615.8 million shares in a rights issue early next year, the bank said in a statement on Saturday.

The new rights issue will give existing shareholders three shares and a warrant for every four shares held, raising at least 800 billion rupiah ($347.8 million), the statement said.

The rights issue has already been registered with Indonesia's Capital Market Regulatory Board (Bapepam) and shares will be listed on February 17 next year.

The bank will hold an Extraordinary General Meeting (EGM) on January 23, the statement said. ($=2,300 rupiah) -- Jakarta Newsroom +6221 384-6364
