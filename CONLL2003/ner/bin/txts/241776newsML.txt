Syria's prime minister to visit China on Sunday.

DAMASCUS 1996-12-07

Syrian Prime Minister Mahmoud Zu'bi travel to China on Sunday for talks on boosting bilateral cooperation especially in the economic field, officials said on Saturday.

They said Zu'bi, invited by Chinese Prime Minister Li Peng, would take along a ministerial team including Salim Yassin, deputy prime minister for economic affairs.

China is involved in several development projects in Syria especially in the textile industry.
