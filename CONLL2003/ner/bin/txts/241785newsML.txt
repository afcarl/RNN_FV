Jordan dinar overnight rate rises to 5.13 pct.

AMMAN 1996-12-07

The weighted average overnight interbank rate on the Jordanian dinar rose sightly to 5.13 percent on Saturday from 5.02 percent on Thursday, the Central Bank said.

It said ten banks traded on the market in 25 reported transactions with a total value of 22 million dinars ($31 million).
