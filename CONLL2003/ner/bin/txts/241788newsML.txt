Lebanon budget deficit 259 bln pounds in November.

BEIRUT 1996-12-07

Lebanon's budget deficit for November dipped sharply to 259 billion Lebanese pounds from 774 billion in October, the finance ministry said on Saturday.

Interest payments were 255 billion pounds in November compared with 740 billion a month earlier.

The ministry issued the following details (in billions of Lebanese pounds) -

					 Nov 96		 Oct 96

 Expenditures			  552		  1,061

  (of which interest payments)   255		    740

 Revenues				293		    287

 Deficit				 259		    774

($1 = 1,554 Lebanese pounds at end November)
