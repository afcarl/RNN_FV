Polish prime minister arrives in Cairo.

CAIRO 1996-12-07

Poland's Prime Minister Wlodzimierz Cimoszewicz arrived in Cairo on Saturday for a visit to include talks with Egypt's President Hosni Mubarak.

Airport sources reported Cimoszewicz's arrival. Egyptian newspapers said on Saturday he would meet Mubarak and Egypt's Prime Minister Kamal Ganzouri for talks on issues including ways to increase economic cooperation.

Al-Ahram newspaper reported Poland and Egypt would sign an agreement ending double taxation, and explore ways to increase increasing trade of $66 million.

The two countries signed a security accord in October aimed at coordinating their efforts on fighting organised crime, guerrill attacks, drug smuggling and forgery.
