Zeroual talks to Mauritanian leader over telephone.

PARIS 1996-12-07

The leaders of Algeria and Mauritania discussed cooperation between the two North African neieghbours during a telephone conversation on Saturday, the official Algerian news agency APS said.

"President (Liamine) Zeroual discussed bilateral relations and issues of common interest with President Maaouya Ould Sid'Ahmed Taya of Mauritania over the telephone," APS said.

They "reaffirmed their will to promote cooperation between the two countries", it added without giving other details.

Algeria and Mauritania are members of the Arab Maghreb Union along with Morocco, Tunisia and Libya.
