Algerian food firm makes $4.5 million profit.

PARIS 1996-12-07

The Algerian food and cereals company ERIAD made a profit of 247.5 million dinars ($4.5 million) in 1995, the official Algerian news agency APS said on Saturday.

It said 40 million dinars would be distributed to workers of the Entreprise Des Industries Alimentaires Cerealieres et Derives (ERIAD) of Tiaret, some 225 km (140 miles) southwest of Algiers.

Two similar firms in the towns of Setif and Constantine, ERIAD-Setif and ERIAD-Constantine, made profits respectively of 288.6 million dinars and 292.9 million in 1995, the agency said. It gave no other details.

($1 = 55 dinars)
