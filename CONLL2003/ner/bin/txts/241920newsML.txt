Pakistan seen deciding sugar tender on Sunday.

KARACHI, Pakistan 1996-12-07

The state-run Trading Corporation of Pakistan (TCP) said on Saturday it was likely to take a decision on Sunday on nine bids for the import of 50,000 tonnes of crystal white sugar for shipment by December 31.

A TCP official said bids had been opened, but no decision was likely before Sunday. He declined to identify the bidders or give details about the prices offered.
