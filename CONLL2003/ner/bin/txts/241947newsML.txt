Pakistan cotton output reaches 5.87 million bales.

KARACHI 1996-12-07

Pakistan's state-run Cotton Export Corp said on Saturday cotton output had reached a provisional 5.87 million 170-kg bales by Thursday in the 1996/97 (July-June) crop year, up from 5.79 million in the year-ago period.

It said said mills had bought 4.18 million bales, up from 3.80 million in the year-ago period.

Private exporters had bought 1,400 bales, compared to 425,900 in the same period last year. Some 1.70 million unsold bales were stocked at ginneries, compared to 1.57 million a year earlier. -- Karachi newsroom 9221-5685192
