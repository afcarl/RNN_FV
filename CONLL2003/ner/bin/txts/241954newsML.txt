Dhaka stocks seen suffering further correction.

DHAKA 1996-12-07

A market correction may continue when trading on the Dhaka Stock Exchange (DSE) resumes on Saturday after the Moslem Friday weekend, brokers said.

"Share prices are expected to go down further. The correction is yet to end," said broker Moshtaque Ahmed.

Brokers said investors were not expected to return until the middle of next week.

The DSE all share price index closed 51.9 points or 1.9 percent down at 2,667.1 on a turnover of 10.4 million taka on Thursday. -- Dhaka Newsroom +880-2-506363.
